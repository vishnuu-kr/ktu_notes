Course Objectives: 
1. Provide a comprehensive understanding of fundamental concepts of sensors and actuators. 
2. Explore various types of sensors, including mechanical, electromechanical, thermal, inductive, and 
gas sensors 
3. Apply knowledge of actuators in different systems.
