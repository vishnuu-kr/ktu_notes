Course Objectives: 
1. To introduce the concepts of electrical measurement systems and instrumentation. 
2. To discuss the principles of operation and construction of basic instruments for measuring 
circuit parameters, magnetic quantities, and passive parameters using bridge circuits, sensors, 
and transducers. 
3. To introduce modern digital instrumentation systems.
