Course Objectives: 
1. 
To provide students with a thorough understanding of algorithmic thinking and its practical 
applications in solving real-world problems. 
2. 
To explore various algorithmic paradigms, including brute force, divide-and-conquer, dynamic 
programming, and heuristics, in addressing and solving complex problems.
