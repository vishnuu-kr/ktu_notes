Course Objectives: 
1. Understand the functions of internal combustion engine components, valve 
actuating mechanisms, air-fuel systems, and Ignition systems. 
2. Study the influence of fuel injection methods, ignition timing, and exhaust gas 
treatment on the performance of IC engines. 
3. Understand the importance and components of cooling and lubrication systems in IC engines.
