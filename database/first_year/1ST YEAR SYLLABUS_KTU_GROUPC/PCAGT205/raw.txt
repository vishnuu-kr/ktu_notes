Course Objectives: 
 
1. To understand the principles of agricultural crop production and protection 
practices of different crops 
2. To understand the role and importance of Agricultural Engineers in crop production 
3. To equip the students with the basic principles of cropping and cultivation 
practices of important crops of Kerala.
