Course Objectives: 
 
1. To equip students with the ability to analyze and solve problems using discrete 
mathematical techniques. 
2. To give a deeper understanding of mathematical logic, set theory, and proof techniques such 
as direct proofs, proof by contradiction, and mathematical induction.
