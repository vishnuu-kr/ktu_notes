Course Objectives: 
1. To impart a comprehensive understanding of knowledge representation, reasoning, and real-
world applications. 
2. To provide the concepts of constructing and applying semantic networks, ontologies, and 
rule-based systems 
3. To help the learner master probabilistic and fuzzy logic techniques for reasoning under 
uncertainty and designing ethical knowledge-based systems for specific domains.
