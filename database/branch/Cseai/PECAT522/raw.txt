Course Objectives: 
1. To help the learners in recognizing and modelling complex patterns and relationships in data 
that might be challenging for traditional algorithms to handle. 
2. To enable the students to create models that can predict future outcomes based on historical 
data, which is valuable in various domains such as finance, healthcare, and marketing. 
3. To equip the learners to perform  classification tasks in a better way, such as image and 
speech recognition, where they can categorize input data into predefined classes with high 
accuracy.
