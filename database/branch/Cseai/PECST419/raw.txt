Course Objectives: 
1. To provide a comprehensive understanding of the fundamental concepts of cyberspace and 
cyber law, enabling them to analyse and address the challenges of regulating and securing the 
digital world 
2. To explain cybercrime, intellectual property, cyber ethics, and ethical issues in emerging 
technologies, enabling them to tackle related challenges effectively. 
3. To give awareness on data protection and privacy in cyberspace, and to learn legal 
frameworks protecting privacy, enabling them to address and manage privacy-related 
challenges effectively
