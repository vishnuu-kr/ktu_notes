Course Objectives: 
1. To provide a comprehensive understanding of the fundamental concepts of graphs and trees. 
2. To learn matching and covering algorithms, connectivity and path effectively. 
3. To equip the learner  to apply vertex coloring, planar graph, Line graph and edge coloring 
effectively.
