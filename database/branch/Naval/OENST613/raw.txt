Course Objectives: 
1. Understand different sources of marine pollution including ships and floating structures, their 
impact on the marine environment. 
2. Select suitable methods to control the marine pollution in different operating conditions 
3. Apply various strategies and methods for marine pollution recovery to minimise 
environmental impact. 
4. Understand role of various international and regional rules and regulations for marine 
environmental protection in design of ships.
