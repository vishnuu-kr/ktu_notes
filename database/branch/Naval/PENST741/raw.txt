Course Objectives: 
 
1. Explain types of welding, welding process and welding parameters used in shipbuilding. 
2. Discern various special welding arrangements in modern shipbuilding. 
3. Discern the radiant welding, underwater welding and robotic welding used in shipbuilding. 
4. Explicate the various types of weld defects and testing in modern shipbuilding.
