Course Objectives: 
1. To introduce regular wave theories and wave spectra to the students and to enable them to 
explain the ship motions in regular waves.  
2. To explain ship motions in irregular waves and ship motion control using stabilisers. 
3. To explain the concept of motion stability associated with ships and standard manoeuvres, 
and to illustrate the heel during turn. 
4. To describe the methods for experimental determination of hydrodynamic derivatives and to 
enable the students to understand the design process for a ship’s rudder
