Course Objectives: 
1. Understand the materials used in ship construction and for the main and auxiliary 
machinery systems onboard. 
2. Study the material properties and heat treatment 
3. Have an overview on thermodynamics and basic theories of heat transfer 
4. Understand the various IC engines and their working. 
5. Select a best suited engine for a particular application
