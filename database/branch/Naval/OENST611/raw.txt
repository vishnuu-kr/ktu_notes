Course Objectives: 
1. Acquire knowledge on various types of floating bodies such as ships. 
2. Develop fundamental concepts of Naval Architecture. 
3. Identify various types of materials used for construction of floating bodies and to identify 
various major and minor structural components of a ship. 
4. Identify various main and auxiliary machineries onboard a vessel.
