Course Objectives: 
 
1. Understand the requirement and various types of marine surveys.  
2. Understand the different types of ship inspection and repair afloat. 
3. Understand ship inspection and repairs in dry dock. 
4. Discern the principles of work estimates and cost estimates based on inspections
