Course Objectives: 
1. Understand the properties of various shipbuilding materials, the framing systems and the 
structural topology of ships. 
2. Discern the major loads on the hull, shear lag and effects and understand classification society 
rules. 
3. Apply classification society rules to finalise the scantlings of ship structure to meet 
longitudinal and transverse strength requirements and draw midship section and shell 
expansion. 
4. Understand the theory of plates and shells applicable to marine structures.
