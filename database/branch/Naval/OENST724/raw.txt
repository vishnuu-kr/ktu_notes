Course Objectives: 
 
1. Understand various types of shipyard layouts, material handling systems.  
2. Discern various production management methodologies in modern shipbuilding. 
3. Discern the various assembly techniques, welding symbols and standards used in 
shipbuilding. 
4. Explicate the various types of outfitting and launching methodologies adopted in modern 
shipbuilding.
