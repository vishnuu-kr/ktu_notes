Course Objectives: 
 
1. Discern thrust development mechanism by screw propellers and its geometry. 
2. Understand hull-propulsion interaction and resulting efficiencies and cavitation. 
3. Understand propulsion system design basics 
4. Understand special types of propulsion systems and propeller strength.

cavitation, Prevention of cavitation

propeller drawing familiarisation.
