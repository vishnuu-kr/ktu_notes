Course Objectives: 
 
1. To introduce the structure of international marine industry and the economical aspects  
in ship design to the student 
2. To provide the students an understanding of the methodologies for preliminary design of 
ships  
3. To provide the students an understanding of the ship design methodologies to develop 
lines plans of typical merchant ships 
4. To enable the student to understand the relevant regulations on freeboard, tonnage and 
stability applicable to ships and to apply these regulations to typical merchant ships
