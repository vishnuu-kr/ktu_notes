Course Objectives: 
1. Understand the historic importance of wood as a shipbuilding material and its relevance today 
2. Understand the use of non ferrous metallic materials in marine applications 
3. Discern the properties of composites used in shipbuilding 
4. Understand the use of advanced and unconventional materials for shipbuilding
