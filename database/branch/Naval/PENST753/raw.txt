Course Objectives: 
 
1. Understand basics of finite element methods 
2. Discern finite element fundamentals like shape functions, convergence, stiffness matrix etc 
3. Understand FEA applications in ship structural analysis 
4. Discern pre processing, processing and post processing considerations in ship structural analysis.

Scope of finite element method in solving  engineering problems, General steps 
in finite element analysis, variational formulations and weighted residual 
methods, Analysis of trusses, beams, and frames, Analysis of plates, Analysis of

Shape functions, Convergence criteria, General equations for calculation of 
stiffness matrix, Derivation of stiffness matrix for truss beam, Plane stress, 
plane strain, axisymmetric elements. 
9 
3 
Ship Structural Analysis using FEM- formulation of plate finite elements, issues 
associated with plate formulation of finite elements. Numerical examples on 
simple plate analysis. One dimensional and two dimensional finite element 
modelling of ship structure 
9 
4 
Preprocessing - preliminary checks, engineering model checks - analysis type 
and assumptions, geometry assumptions, material properties, stiffness and mass 
properties, loads and boundary conditions,  finite element model checks, 
element types, mesh design, substructures and sub-modelling, FE model loads 
and boundary conditions, processing, post processing – methods, displacement 
results, stress results, fatigue and fracture results
