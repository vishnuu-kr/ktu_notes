Course Objectives: 
1. Discern shipbuilding materials, role of classification societies and ship framing systems 
2. Understand the shell and bottom structures of a ship 
3. Discern basic structural drawings and understand the structure of bulkheads and tanks 
4. Understand the structure of decks, superstructures and the end structures
