Course Objectives: 
1. Understand the loads acting on the hull girder, modes of failure and hull girder global 
response 
2. Understand the response of hull girder to wave loads,  
3. Understand the effect of torsion and buckling on hull girder strength and the concept of limit 
state analysis. 
4. Understand the basics of vibration and fatigue as applicable to marine structures.
