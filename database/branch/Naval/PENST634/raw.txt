Course Objectives: 
 
1. Understand the submersible System. 
2. Discern submarine hydrostatics. 
3. Understand submarine hydrodynamics. 
4. Understand the basic structural aspects and engineering systems of a submarine.

objectives, characteristics and development of submersibles, environment –

Submarine hydrodynamics, Resistance, appendage resistance, sway and heave

control system.

hydrostatic control - environmental control system - provision for escape, -
electrical systems.
