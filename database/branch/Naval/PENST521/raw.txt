Course Objectives: 
1. Discern the basic working principle, construction, types, performance characteristics and 
applications of DC generators. 
2. Understand the basic working principle, construction, types, performance characteristics and 
applications of DC motors. 
3. Understand basic working principle, construction, types, performance characteristics and 
applications of AC machines and transformers 
4. Identify the basic principles of instrumentation, measurement standards and types of errors in 
instruments and measurements.
