Course Objectives: 
1. Enable to differentiate between a determinate and indeterminate structures and also to 
analyze continuous beams with and without settlement. 
2. Perform step by step procedure involved in Moment Distribution Method, Accumulate 
knowledge on Principle of Virtual Work and basic theorems in structural analysis. 
3. Understand the concepts of vibration of continuous system such as rod, string, beam, 
shaft, principles of plastic theory and its applications in structural analysis 
4. Remember basic dynamics, understand the basic principles of structural dynamics and apply 
the same to simple structures.
