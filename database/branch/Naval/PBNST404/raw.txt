Course Objectives: 
 
1. 
Discern thrust development mechanism by screw propellers and its geometry. 
2. Understand hull-propulsion interaction and resulting efficiencies and cavitation. 
3. Be competent in the fundamentals of propulsion system design and propeller drawing as a 
project 
4. Understand special types of propulsion systems and propeller strength.
