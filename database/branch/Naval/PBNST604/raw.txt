Course Objectives: 
1. To enable the student to understand the standardization concept, cargo handling arrangements 
in ships and the design process of ship’s rudder 
2. To enable the student to understand the anchoring and mooring systems in ships and to 
determine the numbers and sizes of anchors, chains and mooring ropes 
3. To provide the student an understanding of ship design methodologies to design general 
arrangement of typical merchant ships 
4. To enable the student to understand the relevant regulations on life saving appliances and fire 
fighting appliances and to apply these regulations to typical merchant ships
