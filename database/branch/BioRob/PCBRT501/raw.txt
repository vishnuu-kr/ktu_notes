1. This course aims to make students able to understand the engineering aspects of Robots, 
analyse and design robotic structures and their applications.

Sensors in robots- Touch sensors, Tactile sensor, Proximity and range

Robot kinematics: Forward Kinematics, Inverse Kinematics and Difference; 
Forward Kinematics and Reverse Kinematics of manipulators with Two,

Technological choices - Security. Professional and medical ethics; Robo
