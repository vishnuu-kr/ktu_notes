Course Objectives: 
1. This course covers the principles of momentum, mass, and thermal energy transport as they 
relate to biomedical sciences and engineering. 
2. It explores applications ranging from macroscopic analyses of fluid dynamics in physiological 
systems to microscopic investigations of transport phenomena at cellular and molecular levels 
within biomedical contexts.
