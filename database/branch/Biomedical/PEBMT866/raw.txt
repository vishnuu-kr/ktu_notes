Course Objectives: 
1. To understand the design principles and challenges involved in creating functional 
replacements for various joints and bone fracture-fixation devices 
2. To apply knowledge about the design requirements, materials, and techniques for various 
artificial organs and implants. 
3. To implement 3D printing technology to design and develop biomedical applications.
