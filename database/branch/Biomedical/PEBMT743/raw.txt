Course Objectives: 
1. To provide an in-depth understanding of the technologies and engineering principles behind 
neural prostheses, focusing on visual, auditory, and upper limb prosthetics 
2. To equip students with the knowledge and skills to design, analyze, and implement neural 
prosthetic systems, addressing both technical challenges and practical applications
