Course Objectives: 
1. 
Introduce students to the fundamental principles and concepts of rehabilitation 
engineering. 
2. 
Familiarize students with a wide range of assistive technologies used to support 
individuals with various disabilities. 
3.       Demonstrate how modern technologies, including robotics, web-based tools, and 
other communication aids, can be applied to rehabilitation engineering.
