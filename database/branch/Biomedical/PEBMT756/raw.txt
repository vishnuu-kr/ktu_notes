Course Objectives: 
1. To gain a comprehensive understanding of the fundamental concepts in tissue engineering, 
including cell-extracellular matrix interactions and tissue morphogenesis.. 
2. To learn the design principles and significance of the tissue microenvironment, cellular 
dynamics, and the role of in vitro studies in engineering functional tissues. 
3. To explore the latest advancements in biomaterials, scaffold fabrication techniques, and 
biofabrication strategies for developing engineered tissues.
