Course Objectives: 
1. To provide an overview of Human Factors in Engineering & Design, focusing on optimizing 
human-system interactions to enhance usability, efficiency, and safety. 
2. To understand various human factors aspects essential for designing systems and 
environments that prioritize user experience, operational efficiency, and workplace safety
