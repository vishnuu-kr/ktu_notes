Course Objectives 
1.  Equip the students with an understanding of the fundamental principles of measurement systems. 
     2.  Provide an overview of electronic measuring instruments and introduce the working principle and      
examples of fundamental electronic circuits used in biomedical instruments. 
3. Provide an overview of basic instruments used in measurement of electrical parameters.
