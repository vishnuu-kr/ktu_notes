Role and responsibilities: Definition – Bio-engineering, Biomedical 
engineering, Clinical engineering & Hospital Engineering. Hospital 
Architecture: Planning and design of various department – 
Radiology, ICU, Central Sterilization and Operation Theatres. 
Accreditation protocols: NABH, JCI and AERB certification.

2 
Electrical power systems in hospitals: Stabilized and uninterrupted 
power supply systems, Protective systems- over voltage and over 
current protectors, circuit protectors, Surge protectors, EMI filters. 
Effect of electrical current in human body, Leakage current and its 
types, electric shock- micro & macro shock. Safety measures: Gas, 
fire, electric, and radiation. 
 
9 
3 
Hospital Gas Supply Systems: Centralized gas supply system- air, 
oxygen, nitrous oxide and vacuum –functions, Manifold room, 
principle of production of liquid oxygen. 
9 

 
Sterilization systems in hospitals: principles & techniques of 
sterilization systems,  steam & EO sterilization, Autoclaves & 
Incinerators. 
4 
Procurement & Maintenance of medical equipment: Contract 
procedures- CMC & AMC , preventive maintenance and its types. 
Training of medical staff. IT networking with medical technology: 
Hospital Information Systems (HIS) & Radiology Information systems 
(RIS), Architecture of PACS(Picture Archival & Communication 
Systems)
