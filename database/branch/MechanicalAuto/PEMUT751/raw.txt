Course Objectives: 
1. To understand the Evolution of Data Analytics 
2. To Familiarize Statistical Concepts for Data analytics  
3. To understand Descriptive Analytics and Predictive Analytics Techniques 
4. To Explore the fundamentals of Big Data and Business Intelligence
