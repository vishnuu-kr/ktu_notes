Course Objectives: 
 
1. To impart theoretical and practical knowledge of IC Engine components. 
2. To identify the components, characteristics and functions of the air and fuel supply system 
of internal combustion engines. 
3. Understand the working principle and characteristics of various types of clutches in manual 
and automatic transmission systems
