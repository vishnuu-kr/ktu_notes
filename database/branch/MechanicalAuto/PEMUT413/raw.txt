Course Objectives: 
 
1. Study the constructional details and body styles of car, bus, and commercial vehicles. 
2. Understand the techniques to estimate various forces and moments 
acting on a vehicle. 
3. Understand various materials and techniques used in vehicle body 
construction and painting
