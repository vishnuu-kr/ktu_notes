Course Objectives: 
1. To understand the fundamental principle and working of automotive heating systems, 
refrigeration, and air conditioning systems.  
2. To classify various air conditioning systems, components, and their operational principles, 
focusing on their layout.  
3. To get an overview on the diagnosing, servicing, and maintenance techniques in air 
conditioning systems.
