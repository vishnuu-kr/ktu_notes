Course Objectives: 
1. To understand the basic principles of instrumentation. 
2. Familiarize with various types of sensors and transducers used for measuring physical 
quantities 
3. Gain knowledge of the fundamental concepts of control systems including open-loop and 
closed-loop systems. 
4. Learn about system dynamics, mathematical modelling of physical systems and stability 
analysis  of LTI  systems.
