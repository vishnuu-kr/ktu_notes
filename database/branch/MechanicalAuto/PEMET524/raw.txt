Course Objectives: 
1. To demonstrate appropriate level of understanding on principles of additive manufacturing 
2. To understand the different additive manufacturing technologies. 
3. To choose appropriate materials for additive manufacturing processes 
4. To design prototypes by identifying suitable process with optimum process parameters
