Course Objectives: 
1. To develop the creativity skills among the learners  
2. To impart the knowledge of creative intelligence essential for entrepreneurs 
3. To know the applications of innovation in Entrepreneurship 
4. To develeop innovative business models for business.

Revenue Model, Social Entrepreneurship, Intrapreneurship, biotechnology
