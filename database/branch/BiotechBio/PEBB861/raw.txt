Course Objectives:  
This course will enable students to understand  
1. The developments in the field of cytogenetics, Chromosome banding, chromosomal 
pathologies:  
2. Numerical and Structural aberrations in the chromosomes and their common syndromes  
3. Various technologies related to the field of cytogenetics and apply them in identifying human 
disorders
