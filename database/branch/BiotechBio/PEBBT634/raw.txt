Course Objectives: 
This course will enable students to 
1. To understand the terminologies and basic principles of 
pharmacokinetic and pharmacodynamic involved in the use of drugs. 
2. To know the therapeutic uses and adverse effects of common drugs used for different 
disease conditions, ethical constrains, etc
