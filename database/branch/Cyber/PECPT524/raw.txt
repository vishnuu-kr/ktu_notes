Course Objectives: 
1. To understand various types of input and output devices used in CPS, including sensors, 
actuators, displays, and feedback mechanisms, and their integration into system designs. 
2. To understand the challenges and considerations involved in integrating input and output 
devices into CPS architectures, ensuring seamless operation and effective user interaction. 
3. To illustrate methods for evaluating CPS interfaces, including usability testing, validation 
techniques, and assessing interface effectiveness in real-world scenarios. 
4. To explore emerging technologies and future trends in CPS interface design, preparing 
students to adapt to advancements in the field.
