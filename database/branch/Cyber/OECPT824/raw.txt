Course Objectives: 
1. To understand the fundamental concepts and components of Virtual Reality (VR) systems. 
2. To explain the principles of digital entertainment and development tools in VR, along with 
hardware and software components in AR/VR systems. 
3. To analyze the benefits and challenges of using AR/VR in Cyber-Physical Systems (CPS) 
applications. 
4. To identify and evaluate potential security threats and vulnerabilities in AR/VR CPS 
applications.

1 
Introduction to Virtual Reality (VR): Fundamental concepts and 
components of VR. Input and output interfaces in VR: trackers, sensors, 
digital gloves, movement capture, video-based input, and 3D menus. Visual, 
auditory, and haptic output devices. AR/VR in CPS: Overview of AR/VR 
technologies, Definition and characteristics of CPS, Applications of AR/VR 
in CPS. 
9 

 
 
2 
Interactive Techniques in VR: Body tracking, hand gestures, and 3D 
manipulation, Development tools and frameworks for VR. Application of 
VR in Digital Entertainment: VR technology in film and TV production. 
Physical exercises and games using VR. Demonstrations of digital 
entertainment through VR.  
AR/VR Fundamentals: AR/VR hardware and software components, Types 
of AR/VR (Marker-based, Markerless, Immersive, Non-Immersive),AR/VR 
development tools and platforms. 
9 
3 
Augmented and Mixed Reality: Taxonomy and features of augmented 
reality (AR).Differences between AR and VR. Challenges in AR systems, 
Visualization techniques for AR. Enhancing interactivity in AR 
environments. 
CPS Components and Architecture: Introduction to CPS components 
(sensors, actuators, control systems), CPS architecture and design principles, 
Integration of AR/VR with CPS components. 
9 
4 
AR/VR Applications in CPS: Industrial automation and control, Healthcare 
and medical systems, Smart homes and buildings, Transportation systems.  
Security and Privacy in AR/VR CPS: Security threats and vulnerabilities 
in AR/VR CPS, Privacy concerns and data protection, Secure design and 
development practices.
