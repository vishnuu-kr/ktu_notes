Course Objectives: 
1. To provide students with a thorough understanding of the basic principles and concepts of 
networked control systems (NCS). 
2. To develop the ability to model, analyze, and design networked control systems, including 
switched feedback control and boundary control for damped wave equations. 
3. To teach students how to integrate various components of NCS, including sensors, actuators, 
and communication networks, and address implementation challenges. 
4. To introduce concepts of robust control design, including the synthesis of strictly positive real 
(SPR) transfer functions and ensuring system stability and robustness
