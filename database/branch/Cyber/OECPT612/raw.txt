Course Objectives: 
1. To understand the fundamentals of IoT, its vision, and strategic research directions. 
2. To explore various connectivity technologies and their application in sensor networks and 
M2M communications 
3. To gain practical experience with Arduino and Raspberry Pi, integrating sensors and 
actuators. 
4. To learn about interoperability in IoT systems and software-defined networking.
