Course Objectives: 
1. To understand the knowledge of interfacing sensors and actuators with embedded 
controllers using various circuits and communication protocols. 
2. To understand advanced techniques for interfacing resistive and capacitive sensors, 
focusing on linearity improvement and error reduction. 
3. To understand the principles of miniaturization technology for smart sensors and actuators 
and their fabrication processes.
