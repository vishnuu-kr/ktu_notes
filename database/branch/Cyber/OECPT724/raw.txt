Course Objectives: 
      1. To learn the foundation of human machine interaction.   
      2. To understand the importance of human psychology in designing good interfaces. 
      3. To be aware of mobile interaction design and its usage in day – to – day activities. 
      4. To understand various design technologies to meet user requirements.
