Course Objectives: 
1. To understand the fundamentals of signal processing. 
2. To design and implement filtering and noise reduction techniques 
3. To understand wavelet transform and its applications. 
4. To apply signal processing techniques to sensor data. 
5. To implement signal processing algorithms.
