Course Objectives: 
1. To develop simple programs for 8051 using assembly language programming 
2. To Understand the interfacing of  8051 microcontroller with peripheral devices 
3. To understand the architecture and design concept of embedded systems 
4. To develop  embedded systems based on Arduino.
