Course Objectives: 
1. To understand the fundamental principles of control systems, including the modeling, 
analysis, and design of both linear and nonlinear systems. 
2. To illustrate time domain analysis of first and second-order systems, including transient and 
steady-state responses. 
3. To construct frequency domain analysis using Bode plots and build P, PI, and PID controllers. 
4. To understand state space analysis and represent systems in various canonical forms. 
5. To summarize nonlinear systems using describing functions and classify singular points.
