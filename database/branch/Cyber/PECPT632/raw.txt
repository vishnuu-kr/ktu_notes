Course Objectives: 
1. To understand the principles of networked control systems and their advantages over 
traditional control systems. 
2. To study the impact of network-induced delays and packet losses on control system 
performance. 
3. To analyze different control strategies to mitigate the adverse effects of network issues. 
4. To design and implement networked control systems for real-world applications.
