Course Objectives: 
1. To understand the fundamentals of UX design and its importance in CPS 
2. To apply user research methods to inform UX design decisions 
3. To design intuitive and user-centred interfaces for CPS devices. 
4. To identify security and privacy concerns in CPS UX, design challenges and opportunities.
