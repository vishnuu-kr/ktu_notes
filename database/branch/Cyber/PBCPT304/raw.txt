Course Objectives: 
1. To develop different types of analog circuits using discrete electronic components.  
2. To analyze the basics  of Op-Amp circuits and its applications. 
3. To understand the basic knowledge of logic circuits and enable students to apply it to design a 
digital system. 
4. To understand basic techniques for the design of digital circuits and fundamental concepts 
used in the design of Combinational and Sequential Logic circuits.

Project based learning (8Hrs) 
 The objective of Project based learning to strengthen the understanding of student’s 
fundamentals through effective application of theoretical concepts.  Projects can help to boost 
student’s skills and widen the horizon of their thinking. 
 The ultimate aim of an engineering student is to resolve a problem by applying theoretical 
knowledge. 
 In the beginning, students shall be given an awareness and/or guideline on how to implement 
and learn this course through a project and form groups in the first few weeks. 
 Each group should identify a project that can be done to understand a part of the contents of 
this course and perform a literature survey. 
 Choose a suitable platform for your application and the input output devices to be interfaced. 
 Develop the block diagram and present before the jury within first month after the 
commencement of classes. 
 Implement necessary hardware and software to develop the prototype. 
 Prepare a report and present before the jury for final evaluation.
