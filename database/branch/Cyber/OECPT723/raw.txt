Course Objectives: 
1. To understand the characteristics, requirements, and architecture of cyber physical systems. 
2. To grasp the fundamentals of microprocessor and microcontroller architecture, focusing on 
Digital Signal Processors. 
3. To master the core principles and mathematical foundations of dynamical systems. 
4. To comprehend cyber-physical system software, emphasizing real-time operating systems and 
job scheduling.
