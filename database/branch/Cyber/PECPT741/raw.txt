Course Objectives: 
1. To understand the fundamental concepts and techniques of Operating Systems. 
2. To study fundamental concepts, design principles and hardware considerations 
3. To explore multithreading concepts, processes, threads, and various scheduling structures. 
4. To Gain practical skills in designing and implementing operating system components to 
optimize system performance and reliability.
