Course Objectives: 
1. 
To explain various components of soft computing like fuzzy logic, neural networks and genetic 
algorithm. 
2. 
To understand the fundamentals of Fuzzy sets, including properties, operations and membership 
functions. 
3. 
To summarize the fuzzy inference systems and defuzzification methods. 
4. 
To understand the principles of MOO and Neuro-Fuzzy hybrid systems, including classification 
and characteristics.
