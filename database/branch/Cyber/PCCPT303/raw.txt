Course Objectives: 
1. To understand the fundamental concepts and components of Cyber-Physical Systems (CPS). 
2. To apply modelling techniques, simulation tools, and control strategies for CPS design and 
analysis. 
3. To identify advanced control techniques and security measures in CPS designs.
