Course Objectives: 
1. 
To understand the fundamental concepts, types, and methodologies of intrusion detection 
systems.  
2. 
To analyze various types of network threats and vulnerabilities, assessing their impact on 
security. 
3. 
To evaluate and compare detection techniques for their effectiveness in identifying intrusions. 
4. 
To study and analyze security protocols like SSL/TLS, IPSec, and SSH in securing data in 
transit.
