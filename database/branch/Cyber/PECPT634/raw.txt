Course Objectives: 
1. To learn the fundamental Computer Vision, Computer Graphics and Human-Computer 
interaction Techniques related to VR/AR 
2. To review the Geometric Modeling Techniques 
3. To discuss and Examine VR/AR Technologies 
4. To use of various types of Hardware and Software in Virtual Reality systems and apply 
Virtual/Augmented Reality to varieties of Applications
