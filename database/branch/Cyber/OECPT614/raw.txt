Course Objectives: 
1. To identify User Interface (UI) design principles. 
2. To analysis of effective user friendly interfaces 
3. To apply Interactive Design process in real world applications 
4. To understand various design technologies to meet user requirements.

SEMESTER S6 
ADVANCED CYBER PHYSICAL SYSTEMS LAB 
Course Objectives:  
1.  To introduce basics of cyber-physical system and Industrial revolution 4.0 concepts. 
2.  To develop an exposition of the challenges in implementing a cyber-physical system. 
3.  To analyze the functional behaviour of CPS based on standard modeling formalisms. 
4.  To design CPS requirements based on operating system and hardware architecture 
constraints. 
5.  To understand the concepts involved in Cyber Physical Systems Security.

 a minimum of 4 experiments from Exp. No. 1-6 and 6 experiments from Exp. No. 7-14 are included)

 Study the MQTT protocol. Examine the components of the protocol.
