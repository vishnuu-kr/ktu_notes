Course Objectives: 
1. To Understand and apply advanced cybersecurity concepts and strategies specific to Cyber-
Physical Systems (CPS). 
2. To develop skills to analyze and assess risks and vulnerabilities in CPS environments. 
3. To Implement and evaluate defense mechanisms and security measures to protect CPS 
effectively. 
4. To Explore and assess emerging trends and technologies in CPS security to address 
contemporary challenges.
