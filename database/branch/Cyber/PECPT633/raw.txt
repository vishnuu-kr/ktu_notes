Course Objectives: 
1. Introduction to overview of network security issues, cryptographic algorithms, authentication 
techniques 
2. To understand various Protocols and Transport Layer Security (TLS). 
3. To study various attacks and threats in systems and  security in new generation systems 
4. To understand various Cyber risk assessment methods
