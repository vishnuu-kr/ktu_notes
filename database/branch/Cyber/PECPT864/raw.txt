Course Objectives: 
1. To Understand the principles of user-centered design and the challenges associated with input 
and interaction techniques. 
2. To Explore the concept of Tangible User Interfaces (TUIs), focusing on the use of physical 
objects as input/output devices. 
3. To learn spatial interactions in Augmented Reality (AR) and Virtual Reality (VR) 
environments to enhance immersion in gaming experiences. 
4. To Investigate and design inclusive mobile user interfaces, considering common UI patterns, 
navigation, gestures, and accessibility for users with disabilities.
