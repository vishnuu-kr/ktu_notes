Course Objectives: 
1. To understand the basic principles of special purpose machines (SPMs) in automation. 
2. To explore the design, operation, and control of various SPMs. 
3. To gain knowledge about the integration of SPMs into automated systems. 
4. To study the advancements and emerging trends in SPM technology and to solve industrial 
problems
