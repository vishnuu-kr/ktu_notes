Course Objectives: 
1. To summarize about various computer transmission modes, network models and network 
switching. 
2. To apply knowledge of OSI layers to real-world networking scenarios. 
3. To compare and Contrast the OSI model with other network models (eg: TCP/IP). 
4. To understand the concepts, protocols, and security measures of computer networks.
