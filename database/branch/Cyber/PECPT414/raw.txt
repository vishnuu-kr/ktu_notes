Course Objectives: 
1. 
To explain the steps of experimental design, usability and experimental testing, and evaluation 
of human computer interaction systems. 
2. 
To explain the capabilities of both humans and computers from the viewpoint of human 
information processing. 
3. 
To illustrate an understanding of guidelines, principles, and theories influencing human 
computer interaction.
