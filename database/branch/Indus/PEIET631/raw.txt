Course Objectives: 
1. To enable the students to understand the principles and philosophy of Total Quality 
Management and Six Sigma.  
2. To equip students to apply TQM and Six Sigma principles to real world scenarios.

Introduction to quality and TQM: Quality - definition, quality quantified, 
dimensions of quality. TQM – definition, new and old cultures. Gurus of

Axioms of TQM, Customer satisfaction and Employee involvement: 
TQM axioms - commitment, scientific knowledge and involvement,

retention, employee involvement, motivation, empowerment, team and 
teamwork, suggestion system.
