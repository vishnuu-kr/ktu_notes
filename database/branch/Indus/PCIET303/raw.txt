Course Objectives: 
1. To familiarise the fundamental principles of thermodynamics and its applications. 
2. To introduce principles and applications of steam engineering. 
3. To acquaint the students with working principles of  compressors and gas turbines. 
4. To demonstrate the fundamental heat transfer principles and to describe the basic concepts of 
internal combustion engine operation, combustion processes, and alternative fuels.

Pre ignition and detonation, Factors affecting detonation, Knocking in
