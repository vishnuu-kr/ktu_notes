Course Objectives: 
1. To equip students with the foundational knowledge of metal cutting processes, including 
tool nomenclature, chip formation, and cutting force analysis.  
2. To provide hands-on experience and theoretical understanding of operating conventional 
machine tools and CNC machines, emphasizing their construction, operation, and 
programming.  
3. To familiarize students with the architecture and functions of digital manufacturing 
systems, Programmable Logic Controllers (PLCs), and the key principles of Industry 4.0, 
fostering their ability to implement advanced manufacturing strategies
