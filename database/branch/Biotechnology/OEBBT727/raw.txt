Course Objectives: 
1. To help the students to explore innovative strategies for constructing and executing 
experiments including factorial and fractional factorial designs. 
2. To help the students in extraction of meaningful data from well-designed minimal number of 
experiments at the lowest possible material costs. 
3. To help the students to apply statistical models in anlysing experimental data and optimise the 
response of interest from an experiment.
