Course Objectives: 
 
1. Acquire knowledge on types of immunity, cells, and organs of the immune system 
2. Provide knowledge on essential features of antigen-antibody and their interactions 
3. Explain the concept of hypersensitivity, autoimmunity, and transplantation.  
4. Provide knowledge of immunological techniques.
