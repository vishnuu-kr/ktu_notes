Course Objectives: 
1. To impart foundational knowledge in various types of health data, including electronic health 
records (EHRs), imaging data, genomic data, and patient-generated data.  
2. To teach how computational methods can be applied to improve clinical decision-making, 
enhance patient care, support personalized medicine, and optimize healthcare operations.
