Course Objectives: 
1. To impart the foundational understanding about the principles and concepts behind generative 
AI models, including GANs, VAEs and Transformer-based architectures like GPT. 
2. To educate the learners to apply ethical considerations in the use of generative AI for the 
responsible use and deployment of generative models. 
3. To enable the learners to understand the significance of prompt engineering and cost 
optimization in generative AI.
