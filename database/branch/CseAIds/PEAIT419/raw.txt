Course Objectives: 
1. To discuss the basic concepts and terminologies related to the theory of computation and to 
learn and apply the principles of formal languages, grammars, and automata theory. 
2. To explore Context-Free Languages and Pushdown Automata 
3. To discuss the Chomsky classification of formal languages with a discussion on grammar and 
automata for regular, context-free, context-sensitive, and unrestricted languages. 
4. To discuss the Turing machines' role in defining computability and to explore the limits of 
computation.
