Course Objectives:
●
To provide a comprehensive understanding of Robotic Process Automation (RPA)
concepts and their practical applications.
●
To introduce various RPA tools and their interfaces, focusing on variables,
arguments, and control flow.
●
To prepare students in advanced automation techniques including screen scraping,
data manipulation, selectors, and Citrix automation.
●
To equip students with the skills to handle user events, build assistant bots, and
manage bot deployment and maintenance effectively.

Criteria for Evaluation (Evaluate and Analyse): 20 marks
Suggested mark distribution for the evaluation include:
1. Working RPA implementation - 10 Marks
2. Process Design Documentation and/or Explaination - 5 Marks
3. Presentation and Viva - 5 Marks
Suggested Practice/Application-Based Implementations for Evaluation may include 1 or a
combination of following ideas
1. Setup and Configure a RPA tool and manage the user interface of the tool.
2. Develop and demonstrate a simple RPA workflow using any RPA tool to automate data
entry into an Excel sheet, or Extract data from a PDF and populate a form, or Web
scraping from a site (e.g., extracting news headlines or stock prices), or Email
automation and such types.
3. Create a mini-bot that performs a basic repetitive task like copying files, renaming
documents, filling forms etc, to simulate real-world constraints.
4. Design a Process Design Document (PDD) for a real-life automation scenario in
academics/administration.
5. Give a pre-built bot project with intentional errors or broken selectors. Student must
debug, fix and explain the issues.
