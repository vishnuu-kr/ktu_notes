Course Objectives:
1. To develop an understanding of secure software design principles, threat modeling, and
risk management frameworks essential for building robust and secure systems.
2. To provide practical exposure to cryptographic methods, authentication mechanisms,
and security testing techniques applicable in enterprise and web-based software
environments.
