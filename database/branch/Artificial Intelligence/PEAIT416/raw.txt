Course Objectives:
1. To make awareness about strong theoretical relationships between computer graphics
and multimedia.
2. To understand three dimensional environment representation in a computer,
transformation of 2D/3D objects, basic mathematical techniques and algorithms used
to build useful applications.

Graphics and its applications. Video Display devices- Refresh Cathode
