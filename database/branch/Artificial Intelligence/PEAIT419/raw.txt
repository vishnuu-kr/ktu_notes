Course Objectives:
1.
To introduce the foundational concepts of formal languages and automata, including
finite automata, regular expressions, and context-free grammars.
2.
To develop an understanding of computational models like pushdown automata and
Turing machines, and explore their role in classifying languages and solving
computational problems.
