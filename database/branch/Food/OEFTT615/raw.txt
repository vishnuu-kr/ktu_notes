Course Objectives: 
1. To provide students with a comprehensive understanding of milk types, the various factors 
affecting these compositions, as well as the knowledge of milk's properties and sterilization 
techniques 
2. To provide students with an in-depth understanding of centrifugation and membrane 
separation techniques 
3. To equip students with the knowledge and skills necessary for the manufacture of various 
dairy products
