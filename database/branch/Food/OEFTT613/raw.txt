Course Objectives: 
1. Provide an understanding of various chemical and instrumental methods for analyzing 
food. 
2. Equip graduate students with detailed knowledge of modern techniques used in research, 
development, and inspection of food products in industry, analytical laboratories, and 
government agencies.
