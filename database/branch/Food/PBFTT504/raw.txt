Course Objectives: 
1. To impart knowledge about various categories of food additives their functions, 
permissible limits to be used in various food products. 
2. To gain knowledge on flavour isolation methods and application of flavouring agents in food 
processing

Suggestion on Project Topics 
1. Market study on the food additive – variety and types of food additive in different 
processed foods  
2. Case study on the food additive – Review of literature on health risk caused by food 
additive   
3. Application of food additive in selected  processed food item  
4. Isolation of flavour from selected spices/herbs 
5. Development of seasoning agent and evaluation of seasoning using subjective and 
objective methods
