Course Objectives: 
1. To understand the applications of nanotechnology in food industry 
 
2. Design and tailoring of advanced nanotechnological methods to facilitate emerging methods 
for sustainable food security

Nanotechnology – Definitions – Historical development – Scope and

Module 2: Nanomaterials in Food  
Nanomaterials – Characteristics – Functional properties – Mechanisms of 
action. Types of Nanomaterials used in Food – Organic nanomaterials – 
Inorganic nanomaterials – Composite nanomaterials. Safety and Toxicity of 
Nanomaterials – Regulatory aspects – Risk assessment – Toxicological 
studies.

Module 3: Nanotechnology in Food Processing and Preservation  
Nanotechnology in Food Processing – Enhancement of food texture – Flavor 
improvement – Stability and shelf-life extension. Nanotechnology in Food 
Preservation – Antimicrobial properties – Controlled release of preservatives
