Course Objectives: 
 
1. Students will learn to identify major classes of phytochemicals present in commonly 
consumed foods, such as flavonoids, carotenoids, and phenolic acids. 
2. Students will investigate how various food processing techniques affect the 
concentration and bioavailability of phytochemicals in foods.
