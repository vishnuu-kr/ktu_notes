Course Objectives: 
1. To introduce students to the principles and applications of statistical methods in the 
food processing industry fundamental principles of food process modelling and 
simulation. 
 
2. To provide students with a comprehensive understanding of the different types of 
food process models the role of food process modelling and simulation in the food 
industry and its potential for improving food quality and safety, reducing costs and 
waste, and promoting sustainability.
