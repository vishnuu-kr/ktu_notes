Course Objectives: 
1. To study the basic concepts of management and various forms of business organization and 
trade unions function in professional organizations 
2. To study the planning, organizing and staffing functions of management in professional 
organization 
3. To study the leading, controlling and decision making functions of management in 
professional organization 
4. To learn the organizational theory in professional organization 
5. To learn the principles of productivity and modern concepts in management in professional 
organization.
