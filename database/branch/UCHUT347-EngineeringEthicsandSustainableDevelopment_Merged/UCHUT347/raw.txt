Course objectives:
● Equip with the knowledge and skills to make ethical decisions and implement gender-
sensitive practices in their professional lives.  
● Develop a holistic and comprehensive interdisciplinary approach to understanding engineering 
ethics principles from a perspective of environment protection and sustainable development.
● Develop the ability to find strategies for implementing sustainable engineering solutions.
