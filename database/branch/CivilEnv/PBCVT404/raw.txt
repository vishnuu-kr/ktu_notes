quantity  of water 
2. To study the various types of treatment techniques adopted for a public water supply 
system

Suggestion on Project Topics 
Project: Design a water treatment plant along with a water distribution system (water supply 
system) 
Stage 1: After completing module 1 students should collect the necessary data for the design 
of water treatment plant, complete the quantity estimation and design pumps and pipe 
appurtenances. 
Stage 2: After completing module 2 students should design sedimentation tanks along with 
necessary parameters like coagulant dosage. 
Stage 3: After completing module 3 students should design the filtration and disinfection 
system. 
Stage 4: After completing module 4 students should design a water distribution network and 
network analysis to be done using EPANET. 
Stage 5: The whole project along with layout of treatment plant and distribution network to be 
presented before the panel.
