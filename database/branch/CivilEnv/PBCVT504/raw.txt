Suggestion on Project Topics 
Project: Design a wastewater treatment plant with a suitable disposal system. 
Stage 1: After completing module 1 students should acquire the concept of sewerage system, 
wastewater characteristics. 
Stage 2: After completing module 2 students should design a proper wastewater disposal mechanism 
and preliminary and primary treatment units. 
Stage 3: After completing module 3 students should design the biological treatment units 
Stage 4: After completing module 4 students should design sludge disposal unit and tertiary treatment 
units.. 
Stage 5: The whole project along with layout of sludge treatment plant to be presented before the 
panel.
