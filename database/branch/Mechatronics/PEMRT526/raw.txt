Course Objectives: 
1. Provide students with a deep understanding of crystal imperfections, diffusion, phase 
diagrams, and heat treatment processes, and their impact on material properties and 
mechanical behaviour. 
2. Enable students to explore and apply advanced materials and strengthening mechanisms, 
including fatigue, creep, composites, superalloys, ceramics, and various alloy steels, to 
improve material performance in engineering applications.
