Course Objectives: 
1. To understand the fundamental principles and architectures of computer networks, including 
physical structures, network models, and the interconnection of networks, along with an in-
depth exploration of the OSI model and TCP/IP protocol suite. 
2. To gain comprehensive knowledge of network security mechanisms, including symmetric and 
public-key cryptography, authentication protocols, and firewall technologies, while mastering 
advanced topics in reliable data transfer, congestion control, and routing algorithms in the 
Internet.
