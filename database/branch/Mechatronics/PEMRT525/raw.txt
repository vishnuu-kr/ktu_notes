Course Objectives: 
1. Equip students with a comprehensive understanding of IoT technology, including its 
architecture, enabling technologies, communication models, and protocols, to effectively 
design and deploy IoT systems. 
2. Enable students to address IoT security, privacy, and vulnerability issues, and apply practical 
IoT solutions using platforms like Raspberry Pi for real-world applications such as smart 
cities.
