Course Objectives: 
1. Make students familiar with automation and control technologies in modern manufacturing. 
2. Determine the modern application of automation systems in manufacturing industry. 
3. Provide knowledge on the elements of modern manufacturing systems. 
4. Examine the mechanisms of CMM and FMS.
