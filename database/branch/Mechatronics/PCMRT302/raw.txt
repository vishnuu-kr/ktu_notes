DC MACHINES & TRANSFORMERS  
 DC MACHINES 
Essential parts of a DC machine – EMF equation – DC generator – working 
principle – types of DC generators – no load and load characteristics of DC 
generators – DC motor – working principle – back emf – types of DC motors 
– equations for torque and power (simple numerical problems) 
TRANSFORMERS 
Working principle - Construction - core and shell type - emf equation – 
voltage transformation ratio (simple numerical problems) – concept of ideal 
transformer- transformer on no-load, short circuit and open circuit test on 
transformer (basic 
concept only) – losses in transformer
