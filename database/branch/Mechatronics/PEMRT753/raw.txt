Course Objectives: 
1. Provide students with a comprehensive understanding of the principles, technologies, and 
applications of nano-scale electronic devices and systems. 
2. Develop a solid foundation in the fundamentals of nanotechnology and nanoelectronics and 
understand the implications of nanotechnology on future electronic systems and devices
