Course Objectives: 
1. To equip students with comprehensive knowledge of entrepreneurship, including different 
types of entrepreneurs, forms of business, and the role of entrepreneurship in economic 
development, while fostering an understanding of ethical and social responsibilities. 
2. To develop practical skills in entrepreneurial ideation, business planning, financial 
management, and the operation of MSMEs, with a focus on leveraging government schemes 
and understanding contemporary challenges and opportunities in both Indian and global 
contexts.
