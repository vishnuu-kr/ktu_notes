Course Objectives: 
1. To gain theoretical and practical knowledge in primary manufacturing processes 
2. Provide a detailed discussion on the welding process. Introduce different welding processes 
and advanced processes to be able to appreciate the practical applications of welding. 
3. To understand conventional machining and nonconventional manufacturing 
techniques

chaplets, riser, chills, Defects in castings. Forming- Forging - Extrusion-

Brazing, Soldering, types of solders and fluxes- Adhesive bonding. 
Selection of

indexing.
