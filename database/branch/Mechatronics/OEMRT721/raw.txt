Course Objectives: 
1. To provide students with a comprehensive understanding of robotics, including robot 
structures, classifications, sensors, end effectors, kinematics, and robotic programming, along 
with the basics of PLC systems and their applications. 
 
2. To equip students with practical skills in robotic motion control, sensor integration, and PLC 
programming, enabling effective automation and process control in industrial and research 
settings.
