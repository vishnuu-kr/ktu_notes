Course Objectives: 
1. Equip students with the knowledge and skills to design ergonomic workspaces that reduce the 
risk of musculoskeletal disorders, improve posture, and ensure physical well-being by 
understanding human anatomy, anthropometry, and biomechanics. 
2. Enable students to design optimal lighting, acoustic, and visual environments to minimize 
visual fatigue, eye strain, and auditory discomfort, while improving overall comfort and 
productivity in various work settings.
