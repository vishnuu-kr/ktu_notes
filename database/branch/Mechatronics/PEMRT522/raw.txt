Course Objectives: 
1. Equip students with a comprehensive understanding of engineering design principles, 
including material selection, manufacturability, process capability, and form design. 
2. Enable students to apply sustainable and environmentally responsible design practices, 
focusing on recyclability, energy efficiency, and minimizing environmental impact.
