Course Objectives: 
1. This course introduces Smart Manufacturing, encompassing the integration of advanced 
manufacturing techniques with digital technologies to create intelligent, interconnected 
systems.  
2. Students will learn about the principles, technologies, and applications of SM, preparing them 
to implement and innovate within the modern manufacturing landscape.
