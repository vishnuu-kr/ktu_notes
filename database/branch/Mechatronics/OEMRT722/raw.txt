Course Objectives: 
1. To understand various components of pneumatic and fluid power systems and their 
advantages  
2. Applying the fundamental concepts of fluid power and pneumatic to obtain solution to 
engineering problems.

Basics of fluid power system, Components, Advantages and applications of 
Fluid power systems Fluid properties: specific weight, density and specific

flow, Fluid power symbols.

Hydraulic pumps: Gear, Vane and Piston pumps, Pump performance, 
Characteristics and Selection - Sizing of  pumps
