Course Objectives: 
1. To provide a comprehensive understanding of quality management principles, including the 
evolution of Total Quality Management (TQM) and the contributions of key TQM pioneers, 
along with the application of quality tools and methodologies for continuous improvement. 
2. To develop proficiency in Six Sigma methodologies and their practical applications in both 
manufacturing and service industries, emphasizing process capability, DMAIC phases, and 
achieving customer satisfaction through quality enhancement.
