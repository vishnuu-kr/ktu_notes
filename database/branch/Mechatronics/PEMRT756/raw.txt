Course Objectives: 
1. To provide students with a comprehensive understanding of the static and dynamic force 
analysis of mechanisms, including the application of principles such as D’Alembert’s and the 
design of flywheels for energy efficiency in various engines. 
 
2. To equip students with the skills to analyze and balance rotating and reciprocating systems, 
understand the effects of gyroscopic forces, and solve vibration problems in single-degree-of-
freedom systems, ensuring stability in engineering applications.
