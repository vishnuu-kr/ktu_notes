Course Objectives: 
1. To introduce the elements of control system and its modelling 
2. To introduce methods for analyzing the time response, the frequency response and the 
stability of systems.  
3. This course enables students to systematically study the principles of system modelling

Stability of control systems and Frequency Domain Analysis:
