Course Objectives: 
1. To explain and apply fundamental principles of agricultural instrumentation to monitor key 
parameters 
2. Describe the function and importance of different soil sensors and demonstrate their use in real-
world agricultural scenarios. 
3. To analyze and classify different soil types (sandy, clay, silt, loamy) based on their characteristics 
and evaluate their suitability for various agricultural practices. 
4. Able to evaluate and optimize various agricultural practices, such as irrigation methods and 
greenhouse climate control, using data collected from sensors and environmental monitoring 
systems.
