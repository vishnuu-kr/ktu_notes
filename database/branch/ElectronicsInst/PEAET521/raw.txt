Course Objectives: 
1. Equip students with basic knowledge in data communication networks 
2. Get acquainted with the design constraints of different types of data networks 
3. Familiarize with packet routing methodologies in modern communication networks 
4. Acquire knowledge about various application-level interfaces to data networks
