Course Objectives: 
1. To introduce the fundamental concepts of electronic devices and circuits for engineering 
applications.  
2. To provide comprehensive ideas about working principle, operation and applications of 
electronic circuits. 
3. To equip the students with a sound understanding of fundamental concepts of operational 
amplifiers and its wide range of applications.
