Course Objectives: 
1.  To understand the fundamental architecture of a digital computer. 
 
2. To understand the hardware behind the code and its execution at physical level by 
interacting with existing memory and I/O structure. 
3. To understand the fundamentals of computer system design and to enable the learner to 
extend the features of computer organization to detect and solve problems occurring in 
computer architecture. 
4. To understand the embedded system life cycle management
