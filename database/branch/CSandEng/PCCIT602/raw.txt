Course Objectives: 
1. Aims at exploring various algorithms deployed in offering confidentiality, integrity, 
authentication and non-repudiation services. 
2. Gain in depth knowledge on classical encryption techniques, symmetric and public key 
crypto-system, key exchange and management, and authentication functions. 
3. Gain in depth knowledge on Light weight Cryptography and its relation to the new security 
RFID tags 
4. Apply proactive and defensive measures to counter potential threats, attacks and intrusions
