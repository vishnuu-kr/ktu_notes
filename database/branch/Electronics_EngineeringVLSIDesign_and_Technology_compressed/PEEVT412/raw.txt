Course Objectives: 
 
1. This course aims to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of Linux operating 
systems and scripting languages, specifically Python and TCL, in the context of Very Large 
Scale Integration (VLSI) design 
2. Students will learn how to leverage the power of Linux for VLSI applications, automate 
design processes using Python and TCL scripts
