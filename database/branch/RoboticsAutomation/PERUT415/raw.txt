Course Objectives: 
1. To provide comprehensive knowledge of the wide range of additive manufacturing 
processes, capabilities and materials 
2. To understand the software tools and techniques used for additive manufacturing. 
3. To create physical objects that facilitates product development/prototyping 
requirements
