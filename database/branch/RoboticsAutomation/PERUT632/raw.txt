Course Objectives: 
1. To provide an in-depth understanding of advanced industrial automation systems and 
technologies. 
2. To develop the ability to design, implement, and manage automation solutions for industrial 
applications. 
3. To develop proficiency in programming and using Programmable Logic Controllers (PLCs). 
4. To foster an understanding of the integration of automation with business and manufacturing 
processes
