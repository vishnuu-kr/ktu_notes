Course Objectives: 
1. To introduce the fundamental principles and components of robotic welding systems. 
2. To provide an in-depth understanding of various robotic welding processes and techniques. 
3. To analyze the applications and performance of robotic welding systems in different 
industries. 
4. To equip students with the skills to design, implement, and manage robotic welding systems, 
emphasizing economic, environmental, and safety considerations.
