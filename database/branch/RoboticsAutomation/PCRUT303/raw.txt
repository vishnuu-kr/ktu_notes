Course Objectives: 
1. Understanding the fundamental principles of materials science, developing the ability to 
analyse and characterize materials, learning about the processing and manufacturing of 
advanced materials, and gaining knowledge of the applications of materials in engineering 
and industry. 
2. Understanding various stages of manufacturing, including design, production, and 
assembly
