Course Objectives: 
1. Equip students with the ability to analyze DC and AC circuits using various circuit 
theorems and parameters 
2. Develop an understanding of resonance in series and parallel circuits, including the 
concepts of Q Factor, half power frequency, resonant frequency, and bandwidth, and 
enhance skills in analyzing circuits in the s-domain 
3. Provide comprehensive knowledge of the operating principles, construction, types, 
equations, characteristics, and applications of transformers, DC machines, induction 
machines, and synchronous machines
