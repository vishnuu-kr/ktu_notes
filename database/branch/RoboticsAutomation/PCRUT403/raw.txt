Course Objectives: 
1. Understand the principles and types of manufacturing automation. 
2. Learn about key technologies and systems used in manufacturing automation. 
3. Gain insights into the benefits and challenges of implementing automation in 
manufacturing. 
4. Explore the future trends and advancements in manufacturing automation.
