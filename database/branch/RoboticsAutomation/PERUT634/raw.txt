Course Objectives: 
1. To familiarize the students with the fundamentals of electrostatics, magnetostatics, time-
varying fields and electromagnetic waves. 
 
2. To equip students with expertise in analysing transmission line parameters, and utilizing 
the Smith chart for effective impedance matching and VSWR calculations.
