Hours
1 
Introduction to CAD and CAM: Overview of CAD/CAM, Importance in 
modern manufacturing, Manufacturing Planning and Control, Overview of 
Manufacturing Planning, Key aspects of Manufacturing Control 
Introduction to CIM: Concepts and significance of CIM, CIM system 
components: hardware and software, Production Types and Models, Types 
of production: batch, mass, and continuous, Manufacturing models and 
metrics, Automation and Lean Production, Basic elements of an automated 
system, Levels of automation, Lean Production and Just-In-Time (JIT) 
principles 
9  
2 
Production Planning, Control, and CAPP: Process Planning and CAPP, 
Steps in Computer-Aided Process Planning, Benefits and implementation of 
CAPP, Production Planning Techniques, Aggregate Production Planning, 
Master Production Schedule 
Material Requirement Planning (MRP) and Capacity Planning: 
Introduction to MRP, Techniques for Capacity Planning 
Control Systems and Shop Floor Control: Overview of manufacturing 
control systems, Shop floor control techniques 
Inventory Control and ERP: Basic principles of inventory control, 
Introduction to Manufacturing Resource Planning-II (MRP-II): Overview of 
Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) 
9  

 
 
3 
Cellular Manufacturing and Group Technology: Group Technology (GT) 
and Part Families, Concept of Group Technology 
Classification and coding of part families: Production Flow Analysis, 
Techniques for production flow analysis, Benefits of production flow 
analysis 
Cellular Manufacturing Concepts: Composite part concept, Designing and 
layout of machine cells 
Quantitative Analysis in Cellular Manufacturing: Rank Order Clustering 
Method, Arranging machines in a GT cell using Hollier Method 
Machine Cell Design and Layout: Principles of machine cell design, 
Layout strategies for cellular manufacturing 
9  
4 
Advanced Manufacturing Systems: Flexible Manufacturing Systems 
(FMS), Types and components of FMS, Applications and benefits, Planning 
and control of FMS 
Automated Guided Vehicle Systems (AGVS): Overview of AGVS, 
Applications and benefits, Vehicle guidance technology 
Vehicle Management and Safety in AGVS: Techniques for vehicle 
management, Safety considerations in AGVS 
Industrial Robotics: Anatomy and classification of robots, Robot control 
systems and end effectors, Sensors in robotics 
Robot Accuracy and Applications: Robot accuracy and repeatability, 
Industrial applications of robots 
9
