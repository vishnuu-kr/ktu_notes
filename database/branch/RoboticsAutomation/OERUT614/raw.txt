Course Objectives: 
1. Understand the Fundamentals of Robotics and AI: 
 
2. Utilize supervised and unsupervised learning techniques for robotic perception and 
action. 
3. Develop and evaluate novel AI algorithms for specific robotic applications through 
projects and case studies. 
4. Analyze recent advancements and research trends in AI and robotics.
