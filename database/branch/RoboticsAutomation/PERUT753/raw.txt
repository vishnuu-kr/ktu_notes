Course Objectives: 
1. To understand the fundamental principles and applications of medical robotics. 
2. To explore the design and control of robotic systems used in medical applications. 
3. To examine the role of robotics in surgery, diagnostics, and rehabilitation. 
4. To Analyze current trends and future directions in medical robotics.
