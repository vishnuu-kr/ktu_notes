Course Objectives: 
1. Demonstrate the approaches and techniques to assess and improve process and/or product 
quality and reliability.  
2. Introduce the principles and techniques of Statistical Quality Control and their practical uses 
in product and/or process design and monitoring 
3. Illustrate the basic concepts and techniques of modern reliability engineering tools.
