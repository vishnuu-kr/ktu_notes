Course Objectives: 
1. Understand PLC Fundamentals: Gain proficiency in the principles, operation, and 
programming of PLCs, including ladder logic programming and basic troubleshooting 
techniques. 
 
2. Master DCS Architecture: Learn the architecture, components, and communication protocols 
of Distributed Control Systems (DCS), including their role in industrial automation and 
process control.
