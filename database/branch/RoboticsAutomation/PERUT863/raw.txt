Course Objectives: 
1. To understand the science and technology behind cognitive thinking and to  use it on 
autonomous robots 
2. To familiarize the concepts of robot cognitive models, robot perceptions and 3D 
digital reconstruction 
3. To develop knowledge about the robot programming packages utilized for mapping 
and localization
