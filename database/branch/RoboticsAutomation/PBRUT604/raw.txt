Course Objectives: 
1. Equip students with a solid understanding of the basic principles and significance of soft 
computing techniques, including fuzzy logic, genetic algorithms, and neural networks, and 
their differentiation from traditional hard computing methods 
 
2. Introduce students to the concepts of hybrid systems such as neuro-fuzzy and genetic fuzzy 
systems, and provide a foundational understanding of machine learning techniques, including 
supervised, unsupervised, and reinforcement learning 
3. Enable students to grasp the fundamental concepts of computer vision 
4. Enable students to apply different vision algorithms for industrial applications.
