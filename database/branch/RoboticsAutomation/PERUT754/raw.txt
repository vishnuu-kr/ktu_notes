Course Objectives: 
1. To provide a comprehensive understanding of Industry 4.0 and its significance in the modern 
industrial landscape 
2. To introduce the fundamental concepts and technologies underpinning Industry 4.0, including 
IoT, Big Data, AI, and Cyber-Physical Systems 
3. To explore the practical applications and implications of Industry 4.0 technologies in 
manufacturing and other industries 
4. To prepare students to analyze and implement Industry 4.0 technologies in real-world 
scenarios, considering ethical, legal, and social aspects.
