Course Objectives: 
 
1. Course aims to provide a comprehensive understanding of computer architecture and organization, 
covering fundamental concepts such as bus structures, instruction cycles, and control methods. 
2. Course focuses on practical aspects like assembly language, machine language interpretation, MIPS 
addressing modes, pipelining, and memory systems, equipping students with the knowledge to efficiently 
execute and manage programs.
