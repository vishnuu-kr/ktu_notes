Course Objectives: 
1. 
Study of this course provides the learners a clear understanding of how computer networks from 
local area networks to the massive and global Internet are built, how they allow computers to 
share information and communicate with one another. This course covers the physical aspects of 
computer networks, layers of OSI Reference model, and inter-networking. The course helps the 
learners to compare and analyze the existing network technologies and choose a suitable network 
design for a given system.
