Course Objectives: 
1. To provide a comprehensive Understanding of the drivers and enablers of Industry 4.0 
2. To Appreciate the smartness in Smart Factories, Smart cities, smart products and smart 
services 
3. To explore the various systems used in a manufacturing plant and their role in an Industry 4.0 
world 
4. To prepare the students to understand the role of cyber physical systems in Industry 4.0.
