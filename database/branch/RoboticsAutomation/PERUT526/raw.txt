Course Objectives: 
1. Introduce students to the fundamental concepts of industrial engineering, including its history, 
development, role, and applications. 
2. Equip students with the knowledge and skills to design, analyze, and improve production 
systems, including plant layout, line balancing, and product design. 
3. Develop students' understanding of quality control principles and techniques, including 
statistical process control, sampling inspection, and reliability engineering
