Course Objectives: 
1. Equip students with the fundamental concepts and frameworks of strategic management. 
2. Develop the ability to craft vision statements and set SMART objectives. 
3. Explore different schools of thought in strategic management. 
4. Understand the process, practices, and significance of strategic fit and configuration. 
5. Link core competence to competitive advantage and explore strategies for sustained       
competitive advantage.
