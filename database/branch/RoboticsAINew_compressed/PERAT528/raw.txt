Course Objectives: 
1. Learn how to program CNC machines  
2. To introduce machining principles and processes in the manufacturing of precision 
components and products using non-conventional technologies. 
3. To offer a basic understanding of machining capabilities, limitations, and productivity of 
advanced manufacturing techniques. 
4. Understand the working principle and process parameters of Additive Manufacturing 
processes.
