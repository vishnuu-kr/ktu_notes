Course Objectives: 
1. Ability to analyse and design complex mechanisms involving multiple degrees of freedom 
and constraints.  
2. Mastery of advanced synthesis methods for designing mechanisms that meet specified 
kinematic requirements. 
3. To integrate mechanism design principles with robotics and automation
