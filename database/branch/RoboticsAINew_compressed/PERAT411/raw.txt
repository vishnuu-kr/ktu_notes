Course Objectives: 
1. Understand Soft Computing concepts, technologies, and applications 
2. Understand applications Fuzzy and ANN 
3. Develop Neuro-Fuzzy and Neuro-Fuzzy-GA expert system.

Introduction to soft computing and Artificial Neural Networks: 
Introduction to Soft Computing, Difference between Soft and Hard

architecture, Basic building block of an artificial neuron, Connections,

(basics only) - McCulloch & Pitts model

ANN-Supervised Learning: Neuron as a simple computing element, The 
perceptron, Backpropagation networks: architecture, multilayer perceptron, 
backpropagation learning-input layer, accelerated learning 
in multilayer perceptron, The Hopfield network, Bidirectional associative 
memories (BAM), Radial Basis Function Neural Network

ANN: Unsupervised Learning: Hebbian Learning, Generalized Hebbian

Maps: Kohonen Network. Fuzzy Logic: Crisp & fuzzy sets, fuzzy relations,

statements, fuzzy rules, fuzzy algorithm. Fuzzy logic controller. 
Defuzzification Methods: Lambda cut method.
