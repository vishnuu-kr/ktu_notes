Course Objectives: 
1. To introduce principles of computer organization and basic architectural concepts. 
2. To introduce the programming of a simple digital computer and simple register 
transfer language to specify various computer operations. 
3. To understand computer arithmetic, instruction set design, microprogrammed control unit, 
pipelining and vector processing, memory organization and I/O systems, and multiprocessors
