Course Objectives: 
 
1. To introduce the principles of analog and digital communication techniques 
2. To introduce the principles of computer communication

Geometric representation of signals, Signal space, Gram-Schmitt procedure

Introduction to computer communication: OSI Layering, TCP/IP layering.

Data link layer: Flow control (stop & wait and sliding
