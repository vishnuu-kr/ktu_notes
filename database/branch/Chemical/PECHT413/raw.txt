Course Objectives: 
 
1. To achieve excellence in all areas of an organization by systematically eliminating 
waste, reducing defects and improving customer experiences and satisfaction. 
2. To improve the quality of an organization outputs, including goods and services, 
through continual improvement of internal practices.
