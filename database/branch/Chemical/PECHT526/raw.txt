Course Objectives: 
1. The course is designed to give an overview of numerical methods to students. 
2. The course aims at giving adequate exposure to methods for 
numerically solving algebraic and transcendental equations,

3. To help students to implement these solutions using Excel/ MATLAB or any 
relevant programming environments.
