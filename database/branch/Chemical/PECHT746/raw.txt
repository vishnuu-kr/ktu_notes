Course Objectives: 
1. The analysis of the phenomena of heat and mass transfer is important in engineering design 
applications. Heat and mass transfer processes are modelled by similar mathematical 
equations in the case of diffusion and convection. It is thus more efficient to consider them 
jointly. 
2. Besides, heat and mass transfer must be jointly considered in some cases like humidification 
and drying. This course also explains how to solve engineering problems involving 
conduction, convection, diffusion mass transfer, interphase mass transfer, simultaneous heat 
and mass transfer.
