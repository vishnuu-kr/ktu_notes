Course Objectives: 
1. Provide fundamental understanding of basic principles of heat transfer and 
various modes of heat transfer. 
2. Solve heat transfer problems using the principles and correlations for different modes of heat 
transfer. 
3.  Design and estimate heat transfer rate for heat transfer equipment like heat 
exchangers and evaporators.

1 
Basic Concepts - Overview of applications of heat transfer in different 
fields of engineering, modes of heat transfer - conduction, convection and 
radiation. 
Material properties of importance in heat transfer - thermal 
conductivity, thermal diffusivity, specific heat capacity, isotropic and 
anisotropic materials, thermal conductivity of solids, liquids and gases – 
comparison between them, effect of temperature on thermal conductivity. 
Conduction Heat Transfer - General heat conduction equation in cartesian 
coordinates (derivation required), Fourier heat conduction equation, 
conduction through systems of constant thermal conductivity - conduction 
through plane, cylindrical and spherical wall, combined boundary condition 
systems (conduction-convection systems), conduction through composite 
slab - multi-layered plane, cylindrical and spherical shells, electrical 
analogy to heat flow, overall heat transfer coefficient. 
Thermal Insulation - Analysis of critical radius of insulation for cylinders 
and spheres, critical thickness of insulation, concept of optimum thickness 
of insulation, concept of thermal contact resistance. 
Unsteady State Heat Conduction - Analysis of transient heat flow with

Convection - Mechanism, boundary layer concepts, thermal and velocity

The convective heat transfer coefficient, dimensionless numbers in 
convection. Dimensional analysis - Buckingham's pi theorem, its 
application of dimensional analysis to forced convection. 
Forced Convection – correlation equations for estimation of convection 
heat transfer coefficient- laminar and turbulent flow in a circular tube, 
laminar and turbulent flow over flat plates, numerical problems of practical 
interest. 
Natural Convection - Dimensional analysis, natural convection from 
vertical and horizontal surfaces under laminar and turbulent conditions for 
flat plates and cylinders under constant heat flux and constant wall 
temperature conditions, physical significance of Grashof and Rayleigh 
numbers, numerical problems of practical interest. 
Analogy between momentum and heat transfer- Reynolds, Prandtl, Colburn 
analogies. 
Radiation heat transfer - thermal radiation, spectral emissive power, total 
emissive power, emissivity, radiative properties- Emission, irradiation, 
radiosity, absorptivity, reflectivity and transmissivity, concept of black and 
gray bodies. 
Black body radiation -laws of black body radiation, view factor, radiation 
between two large parallel gray planes-derivation of expression for rate of 
radiant energy exchange. 
Problem solving in convection and radiation using EXCEL/ Matlab/ 
SCILAB/ PYTHON etc. – only for selfstudy /microproject/ assignment

3 
Boiling and Condensation - pool boiling, regimes of boiling, correlation 
for critical heat flux for nucleate pool boiling-Condensation - Drop and film 
type condensation – Nusselt equation to determine the heat transfer 
coefficient in film condensation (no derivation). 
Evaporation - short tube vertical or calandria type evaporators- Evaporator 
auxiliaries -steam traps, vacuum devices and entrainment separators. Single

and economy of evaporators, factors affecting the performance of 
evaporators, effect of liquid head and boiling point elevation, material and

Multiple effect evaporators - material and energy balances, Different 
feeding arrangements in multiple effect evaporators – their merits and

Problem solving in boiling and evaporation, evaporator design using

4 
Heat exchangers - classification according to construction features - 
tubular heat exchangers (shell and tube, double pipe), plate-type heat 
exchangers, extended surface heat exchangers, classification according to 
flow arrangements - single-pass exchangers, multi-pass exchangers. 
Concept and types of fouling - fouling factors, determination of overall 
heat transfer coefficient with and without fouling, derivation of expression 
for LMTD, concept of correction factor logarithmic mean temperature 
difference. 
Heat exchanger analysis using LMTD method in parallel flow, counter 
flow, cross flow and multi-pass heat exchangers, temperature – distance 
plots for different flow arrangements in single and multi-pass heat 
exchangers. 
NTU method of heat exchanger calculation, determination of area, length, 
number of tubes required for a given duty in different configurations using 
LMTD and NTU method of analysis. 
 
Problem solving in heat exchanger design using EXCEL /Matlab / SCILAB 
/ PYTHON etc. – only for selfstudy /microproject/assignment
