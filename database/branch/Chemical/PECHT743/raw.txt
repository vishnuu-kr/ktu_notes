Course Objectives: 
1. Impart the basic concepts of safety in Process Industries. 
2. Develop an understanding about safety practices in industries. 
3. Develop an understanding about hazard identification, emergency planning and hazard evaluation.  
4. Develop an understanding about safety practices in industries and emergency planning. 
5. Incorporate the concepts of Inherent Safety and Process Intensification and models for Fire, 
Explosion and Toxic gas dispersion.
