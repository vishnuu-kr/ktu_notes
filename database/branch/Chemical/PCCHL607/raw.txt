Course Objectives: Process Simulation Lab prepares students for real-world challenges, 
fosters creativity, and enhances their problem-solving abilities. Fundamental concepts of 
mathematical models in chemical processes; simulation of chemical units such as flash drum, 
heat exchanger, continuous stirred tank reactor, plug flow reactor, distillation column, 
absorption column and so on; and sensitivity analysis are covered in detail. 
1. To equip students to conceptualize and develop flow sheet for chemical engineering 
process. 
2. To introduce different commercial softwares to simulate the chemical processes. 
3.  To integrate and apply knowledge from prior courses to solve open-ended problems. 
4.   To interpret the results of the simulation problems. 
Experiments can be done using Excel, MATLAB/ Scilab, UniSim Design/ DWSIM or any other 
chemical process simulation software.
