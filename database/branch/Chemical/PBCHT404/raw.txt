Course Objectives: 
 
1. Develop the ability to identify and analyse wastewater sources, understand wastewater 
characteristics and assess the impact. 
2. Impart comprehensive knowledge of the principles, technologies, and methodologies in 
wastewater treatment, solid waste management and air pollution control. 
3. Instill the insight of sustainable development and optimal use of water, land and air resources.

Suggestion on Project Topics 
 
1. Collect sample from any polluted water source or effluent from nearby industry like food 
processing, metal polishing, pulp and paper etc., analyze the physical, chemical and biological 
characteristics and suggest a treatment process with required primary, secondary and tertiary 
equipment to treat the effluent according to the disposable standards specified by legislation. 
Use modelling and simulation software to design the process. 
2. Collect air sample from flue gas, urban polluted area, analyze air quality index and design 
suitable air quality monitoring/control equipment. 
 
3. Collect any solid waste sample (like e-waste, hazardous waste, MSW), analyze the 
characteristics and suggest/design treatment process/equipment. 
 
OR 
Divide the project into 3 parts as follows Project I 
(15 marks): Do any one project from the given list 
 
1. Identify socially relevant / environmental issue in your nearby locality, explore it and 
suggest remedial measures. 
 
2. Identify a waste water source in nearby locality and study the physical, chemical 
characteristics. 
 
 
 
 

 
 
3. Collect air sample from flue gas, urban polluted area, analyze air quality index. 
 
4. Collect any solid waste sample (like e-waste, hazardous waste, MSW), analyze the 
characteristics. 
 
Project II (10 marks): Do any one project from the given list 
 
1. Design grit chamber and sedimentation tank for a specific wastewater pollution problem. 
 
2. Design an activated sludge reactor for a specified wastewater pollution issue. 
 
3. Design trickling filter and rotating biological contactor separately to address a specific 
wastewater pollution challenge. 
 
4. Design any one anaerobic reactor for waste water treatment. 
 
5. Design of an anaerobic digester for a given solid waste management issue. 
 
6. Design any two air pollution controlling equipment. 
 
 
 
Project III (5 marks): 
 
1. Design of any single pollution controlling equipment for a specific problem using 
software like DWSIM, BIOWIN, SUMO, etc. or excel.
