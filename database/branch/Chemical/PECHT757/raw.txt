Course Objectives: 
1.   This course focuses on economic and cost analysis of engineering projects, giving insights on 
tools used for cost estimation, profitability analysis and taking decisions among alternatives.  
2.   The basic purpose of this course is to provide a sound understanding of concepts and principles of 
engineering economy and to develop proficiency with methods for making rational decisions 
regarding problems likely to be encountered in professional practice.
