Course Objectives: 
1. Equip students with the fundamental knowledge and analytical skills necessary for designing, 
analysing, and optimizing chemical processes in various industries. 
2. Enable the students to understand and analyse ideal conditions in various aspects of chemical 
processes and thus develop more efficient and effective engineering solutions.
