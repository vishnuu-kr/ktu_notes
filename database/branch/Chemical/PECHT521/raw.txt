Metabolic pathways and energetics of the cell: Metabolic reaction

Biochemical reactors-specifications Continuous stirred tank bioreactor, 
bubble column bioreactor, Airlift bioreactors, Fluidized bed bioreactors, 
Packed bed bioreactors, and photo bioreactors. Types of reactors for 
sterilization. Fermentation technology, medium formulation, design and 
operation of a typical aseptic, aerobic fermentation process. Concept of 
biosensors. Physical and chemical sensors, gas analysis sensors, On-line 
sensors and offline sensors.
