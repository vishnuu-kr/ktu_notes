Course Objectives: 
1. Explain importance of PSM and the basic requirements in PSM standards  
2. Explain the element required by OSHA standard: Process Safety Information and Process 
Hazard Analysis  
3. Explain the elements required by OSHA standard: Operating Procedures, Employee 
Participation, Training, Contractors, Pre-start-up Review. 
4. Explain the elements required by OSHA standard: Mechanical Integrity, Hot work Permit, 
Management of Change, Incident Investigation, Emergency Planning and Response, 
Compliance Audits and Trade Secrets
