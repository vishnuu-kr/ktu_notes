Course Objectives: 
1. To enlighten the engineering students about the scope and cost of corrosion. 
2. To make them understand the various types of corrosion under normal to severe corrosive 
environments in manufacturing industrial sectors.   
3. To develop the skills among the students about corrosion testing of materials and to 
familiarize the students in selection of materials, corrosion monitoring and prevention.  
4. To develop the specialized skills in corrosion control in different engineering materials and 
industries.
