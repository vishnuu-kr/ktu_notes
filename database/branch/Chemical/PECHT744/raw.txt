Course Objectives: 
1. Equip students with the skills to map and analyze process flows, define input and output 
parameters, and implement automation 
2. Understand automatic control principles, enabling them to design and optimize control 
systems effectively. 
3. Apply advanced control strategies and integrate them into automated systems to enhance 
process reliability and performance in industrial applications.
