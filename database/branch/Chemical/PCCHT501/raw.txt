Course Objectives: 
1. To impart fundamental concepts of mass transfer operations such as 
distillation, liquid extraction, leaching and membrane separation processes.  
2. To explain how to quantify, formulate, and solve engineering problems 
involving different mass transfer operations like distillation, liquid-liquid 
extraction, leaching and membrane separation processes.

Extraction –applications, ternary   equilibria   on   triangular coordinate

Single-stage   and   multistage   extraction operations - Calculations   for  
immiscible   systems   and   partially miscible systems. 
Construction and working of mixer -settler cascades, sieve-tray columns,

Membrane separation processes – classification – types of membranes:
