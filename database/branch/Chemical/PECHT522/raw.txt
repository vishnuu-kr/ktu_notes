Course Objectives: 
1. To bring an optimal balance between natural gas production, natural gas processing 
and gas transportation, an extensive treatise on natural gas engineering, both upstream 
and gas refining processes with key equipment and facility design. 
2. Awareness of the current status of production of natural gas through unconventional 
sources/technics and the applications of natural gas.
