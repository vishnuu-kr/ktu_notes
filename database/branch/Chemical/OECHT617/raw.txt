Course Objectives: 
1. To study the various types of environmental pollution 
2. To study the impact of various types of pollutants and their assessment techniques

Introduction: Legislations, laws and acts relevant to Environmental 
protection in India -The environment (protection) Act-1986, The Air 
(prevention and control of pollution) Act 1981, The Water (prevention and 
control of pollution) Act-1974, National environmental policy 2006. 
Classification of Pollution and Pollutants- 
Water pollution: Major Pollutants of Water and their effects, Physical, 
chemical and biological characteristics of water, Water Quality standards 
Air Pollution: Primary and Secondary Pollutants, Impact of air pollutants 
on human, vegetation and environment, Ambient Air Quality Standards, 
meteorological factors affecting air pollution.

2 
Solid waste: Classification and sources of Solid Waste, Characteristics of 
Solid Waste. 
Land Pollution: Effects of urbanization on land degradation, Impact of 
Modern Agriculture on Soil, pesticide pollution, Effect on Environment. 
Noise Pollution: Sources of Noise, Effects of Noise, measurement of noise, 
Equivalent sound pressure level, Control measures.

EIA, Impact analysis
