Course Objectives: 
1. To apply chemical engineering principles to design and analyze refinery processes.  
2. To gain knowledge of the various stages of crude oil refining, including distillation, 
cracking, and reforming. 
3. To provide students with a comprehensive understanding of the petrochemical 
industry, its processes, and the underlying principles
