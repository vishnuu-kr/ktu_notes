Course Objectives: 
1. To understand the blockchain applications in a structured manner.   
2. To learn the concepts of consensus and mining in Blockchain through the Bitcoin network.  
3. To understand Ethereum and develop-deploy smart contracts using different tools and 
frameworks  
4. To impart knowledge in block chain techniques and able to present the concepts clearly and 
structured.
