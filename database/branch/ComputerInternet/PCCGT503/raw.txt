Course Objectives: 
1. To develop their ability to analyse and compare the architectural features of the 8086 
microprocessor and 8051 microcontroller. 
2. To develop their ability to understand the basics of the Arduino platform and its significance 
in IoT projects. 
3. To develop their ability to integrate and program IoT Solutions with Raspberry Pi                                           
4. To develop their ability to develop applications using NodeMCU.  
5. To develop their ability to interface microcontrollers and microprocessors with IoT.
