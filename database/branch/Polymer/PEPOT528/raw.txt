Course Objectives: 
1. The field of natural gas engineering is very much important for petroleum engineers specializing 
in gas processing technology.  
2. The course outlines an optimal balance between natural gas production, natural gas processing and 
gas transportation. An extensive treatise on natural gas engineering, both upstream and gas refining 
processes with key equipment and facility design will be covered. 
3. This course will also highlight the current status of production of natural gas through 
unconventional sources/ techniques and the applications of natural gas.
