Course Objectives: 
1. Objective of this course is to explain the importance and applications of biopolymers and 
biomedical materials with its research and development.

Biomaterials – Definition, requirements, Classification, Bioactive, Bio 
inert and Bioresorbable biomaterial, sterilisation methods of biomaterials. 
Typical examples for polymers used as biomaterials: ultrahigh molecular 
weight polyethylene, poly (methyl methacrylate), hydrogels, silicone, 
polyurethanes, polylactic acid, polyglycolic acid and its copolymers, 
polycaprolactone, chitin and chitosan.

silicone implants, polymer membranes, polymer skin, contact lens types and 
materials, biodegradable polymers and its applications, polymeric tissue 
engineering scaffolds.

mouth protectors, restorative materials, dental adhesives, dental impression
