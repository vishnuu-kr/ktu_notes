Course Objectives: 
 
1. To make the students aware of types of plastic materials and its properties 
2. To make the students capable to estimate the difference in thermal and physical behaviour of 
plastic materials. 
3. To understand the processing behaviour of plastics. 
4. To understand the applications of plastics.
