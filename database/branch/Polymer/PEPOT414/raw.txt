Course Objectives: 
 
1. To make the students aware of fermentation and its upstream and 
downstream processing. 
2. To make the students capable to understand production of commercially 
important primary and secondary metabolites. 
3. To understand the biopolymers, vaccines and monoclonal antibodies. 
4. To understand the modern trends in use of enzymes, biofertilizers, bioremediation techniques.
