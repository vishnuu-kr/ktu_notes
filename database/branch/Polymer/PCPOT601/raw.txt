Course Objectives: 
1. To provide in depth knowledge about steps involved in polymer product design with the basic rules 
and formulations. 
2. Student will be able to design formulations of typical rubber products, its processing, and curing 
process. 
3. Students will be able to design plastic products with the specifications as per the testing standards.

design, functional design, aesthetic design, effect of fillers on properties

films, sheets, bottles, water tanks, wires and cables, Standard test methods

packing containers.

Rubber bearings, bridge bearings, rubber seals, static and dynamic seals,
