Course Objectives: 
1. This course is designed to provide students the fundamental concepts of polymer gels and 
electrolytes 
2. To understand the preparation of polymer gels and polymer electrolytes as it is important to 
fabricate various products for various applications. 
SYLLABUS 
Module 
No. 
Syllabus Description 
Contact 
Hours 
1 
Introduction to polymer Electrolytes- Definition, composition, types of 
polymer electrolytes- solid polymer electrolytes, liquid polymer electrolytes, 
gel polymer electrolytes, ionic polymer electrolytes. Common polymers 
included as polymer electrolytes; different interactions seen in polymer 
electrolytes. Different synthesis method of polymer electrolytes, advantages 
and disadvantages of polymer electrolytes 
9  
2 
Properties & Applications of Polymer Electrolytes- ionic conductivity, ion-
transfer mechanism, chemical properties, mechanical & thermal properties, 
electro-chemical properties, electrochemical characterization by cyclic 
voltammetry and electrochemical impedance spectroscopy, optical properties, 
dielectric properties, solvation and desolvation kinetics, adhesion & cohesion, 
interfacial tension, contact angle.Applications in energy storage device, 
electrochemical sensors, optoelectronic devices and biomedical applications 
9  

 
 
3 
Polymer Gels: Polymer hydrogels, aerogels, macro, micro and nanogels, 
organogels. Cellulose based polymer gels, polysaccharide-based polymer 
gels, graphene oxide-based polymer gels, Functional polymer gels. 
Importance of crosslinking in gels. Examples of crosslinking agents. Various 
synthesis methods for polymeric gels. Factors influencing network formation 
in gels, different interactions in gels. 
9  
4 
Properties & Gelation- rheological properties, swelling study of gels, 
mechanical properties and its characterisation, role of gelation, factors 
influencing gelation. Biocompatibility of gels and its applications in various 
biomedical fields. Gels as shape memory materials, polymer gels for energy 
storage devices.  
9
