Bioreactors - Function, description, working, advantages and 
limitations of stirred tank, airlift, airlift pressure cycle bioreactor,

maintenance, calculation of batch reaction time from ideal system for 
enzyme reaction and cell culture, batch reaction time with enzyme 
deactivation, calculation of total batchtime. Ideal continuous flow

biomass productivity, mean residence time
