of motion, transport equations, and equilibrium states 
3 
Chemical Reaction Engineering by Prof. Kannan Moudgalya, IIT Bombay. 
This course covers models of reactors, including mixing with reaction, reversible reactions, and 
steam jacketed vessels 
4 
Process Equipment Design by Prof. M. K. Jha, IIT Roorkee. 
This course includes models of separation processes like multicomponent flash drums and 
distillation columns
