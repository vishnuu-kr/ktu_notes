1. After the completion of this course, students should achieve sufficient knowledge about the basics

Project based learning (8 hrs) 
 
1. On the first class before starting the first module, direct the students to prepare a polymer-based 
paint formulation 
2. Students shall refer water based and solvent based paint formulations of different companies. 
3. Analyze the importance of different components in a paint formulation 
4. Analyze the different additives and its importance based on their function. 
5. Design a suitable equipment for pigment dispersion and compare different pigment dispersion 
methods. 
Manufacture water and solvent based paints and study different mechanical properties using 
various testing methods after the completion of all 4 modules. 
6. 
Prepare a project report based on their project work.
