Course Objectives: 
1. To provide in depth knowledge from methodology of impact analysis, socio impact analysis, air 
quality impact analysis and water quality impact analysis. 
2. Understand the fundamentals of environmental impact and factors for consideration in assessing 
environmental impact analysis.
