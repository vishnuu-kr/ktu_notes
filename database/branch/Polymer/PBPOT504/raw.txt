Course Objectives: 
1. 
To explain the Industrial manufacturing of tyres along with its research and development 
2. 
To introduce students about the machineries used to manufacture various types of tyres and also 
the formulation for various tyre components, its processing, building and curing process. 
3. 
After this course the student will be able to explain the tyre grip mechanism, tyre design features 
and tyre testing methods

Suggestion on Project Topics 
● 
After teaching the students the fundamentals of Tyre Technology (Module 1) 
● 
Faculty can direct the students to design a tyre according to a particular need.  
● 
Ensure that it is suitable for the course.  
● 
Students should prepare the plan which includes the appropriate compounding ingredients 
and formulations for various tyre components in the due time of Module II. 
● 
Students can refer the appropriate code of practices.  
● 
Students can fabricate different components of tyre with the help of suitable machineries. 
● 
Assembling of tyres and curing of tyres should also be done according to the tyre type in 
the due period of Module III. 
● 
Any tyre testing methods can also be performed by the student on the tyre he/she has 
designed during the time of Module IV. 

 
 
● 
Students should submit the report to the faculty.
