Course Objectives: 
This course aims to: 
1. Introduce the fundamentals of VLSI fabrication and the implementation of digital circuits 
using MOS transistors, covering MOSFET operation, MOS circuit design principles, including 
layout design rules and combinational logic circuits. 
2. Explore basic circuit concepts and review power dissipation in CMOS circuits, focusing on 
strategies for power reduction.
