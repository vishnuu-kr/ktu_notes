Course Objectives: 
1.   Develop a comprehensive understanding of both analog and digital communication systems, 
including fundamental principles, modulation techniques, and key components of advanced 
communication systems like microwave, satellite, and optical communications. 
2.   Gain proficiency in digital modulation schemes, understand the evolution of mobile 
communication technologies from 1G to 5G, and learn about modern wireless 
communication systems and multiple access techniques.
