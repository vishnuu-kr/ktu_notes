Course Objectives: 
1. Understand the basic concepts and techniques employed in broad area of human factors 
engineering focusing on anthropometric principles in design of work places and to optimize 
the interaction between human and systems. 
2. Integrate human factor considerations in the design of medical devices and health care tools 
for improved user experience and safety.
