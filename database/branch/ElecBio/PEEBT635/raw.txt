Course Objectives: 
1. Gain comprehensive knowledge of the basic principles of robotics, including core components such as 
sensors, actuators, and drive mechanisms.  
2. Examine the kinematics and dynamics of robotic systems, focusing on their practical applications in 
healthcare also develop and implement programs for controlling robots to improve medical procedures 
and patient care.
