Introduction: Introduction to nanotechnology and bio nanotechnology, time

Analytical tools in nanobiotechnology: Tools in Nanobiotechnology: 
Microscopy and imaging techniques such as SEM, TEM, AFM and STM

Applications of nanobiotechnology: Applications of nano-biotechnology in

medicine . Nano-biotechnology in cancer, nanotherapeutics: gene therapy 
and immunotherapy. New materials and devices in medicine-implants and 
coatings, chemotherapy administration, nanobots. Future goals& Potential 
effects on global economics- Sustainable agriculture, sensors, nano carriers
