Course Objectives: 
1. Recognise the suitability of various materials for biomedical applications by evaluating their 
inherent properties, such as biocompatibility, mechanical strength, and degradation kinetics. 
2. Proficiency in utilizing design principles and fabrication techniques to produce biocomposite 
materials, including conventional composite processing methods and advanced 3D printing 
technologies.
