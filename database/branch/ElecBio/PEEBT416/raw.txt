Course Objectives: 
1. Develop proficiency in using various statistical inference techniques, including hypothesis 
testing, confidence intervals, and p-value calculations, to make informed decisions based on 
sample data. 
2. Acquire the skills to design experiments, collect data, and apply ANOVA techniques to assess 
the significance of differences between group means.
