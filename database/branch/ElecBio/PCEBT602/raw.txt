Course Objectives: 
1. Students will acquire knowledge of the physics and technology behind radiological imaging 
and radiotherapy. 
2. Students will be able to understand principles of radiation physics, including the interaction of 
radiation with matter.

Radiographic system: X-Ray production, interactions in the anode- 
Bremsstrahlung and characteristic radiations. Stationary and rotating anode 
X-ray tubes. Electrical circuit for an X-ray unit- Autotransformer, Timer, HT 
generator, rectification, filament circuit, meters, automatic exposure controls, 
generator ratings, factors influencing the X ray- spectra, beam quantity and 
quality, scattered radiation control in radiography- collimators and grids.

silicon TFTs. Direct-capture DR-active-matrix array- amorphous selenium.
