Course Objectives 
1. This course aims to develop the skill of analyze and design of different types of analog circuits like 
amplifiers, power amplifiers, differential amplifiers, oscillators and pulse circuits using discrete 
electronic components.

Junction Field Effect Transistors and MOSFETs: Structure, principle of 
operation and characteristics of JFETs. Structure and physical operation &

amplifier Circuit (CS configuration)-Design.

Power amplifiers: classification, class A and Class B power amplifier, 
Class AB, Class C and Class D Power Amplifiers efficiency and distortion 
(no analysis is required). Feed-back amplifiers: General feedback 
structure, properties of negative feedback, and four basic feedback 
topologies. Sinusoidal oscillators: criterion for oscillation, basic principles 
of RC and crystal oscillators.
