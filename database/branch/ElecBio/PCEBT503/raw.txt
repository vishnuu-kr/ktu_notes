Cardiac Assist Devices:  
Cardiac pacemakers: different modes of operation, external and 
implantable pacemakers. Defibrillators: AC and DC defibrillator – basic 
principle. Cardioverter defibrillator – block diagram. Implantable 
defibrillator and automated external defibrillator (AED)– functional block 
diagram.  Catheterization- IABP, Stents.  
6 
2 
Respiratory Aids: Ventilators - Block diagram, ventilator settings, types of 
ventilators-Pressure, Volume, Time controlled, Flow, Patient Cycle 
Ventilators, Modes of ventilation: Non-Invasive- CPAP, BiPAP; Invasive 
modes - Controlled, Assisted, SIMV, APRV, Pressure Support; Flow 
sensors and FiO2 sensor.   Anesthetic machines - Block diagram and 
working Gas blending and vaporizers, Anesthesia delivery system, breathing 
circuit. Computer aided anesthesia control. 
8 
3 
ExtraCorporeal Devices: Dialysis machine - haemodialysis unit, block 
diagram and working. Heart lung machine (HLM) - Heart lung machine 
(HLM) – principle of operation-functional block diagram -types of 
oxygenators. Extracorporeal membrane oxygenation (ECMO) machine. 
Lithotripsy - Concept of lithotripter systems, Extracorporeal shock wave 
lithotripsy (ESWL). Drug delivery systems – Different approaches for 
12 

 
 
drug delivery, Drug infusion devices -Gravity Drip Systems, Infusion 
pump, Syringe pump, Implantable pumps, Patient Controlled Analgesia 
(PCA) Pumps, Elastomeric Infusers. Recent developments in drug infusion 
systems. 
4 
Diathermy, Medical Stimulators & Special Diagnostic Aids: Surgical 
diathermy - Short-wave diathermy, Ultrasonic therapy, Interferential 
current therapy, Neuro drills, Neuro navigation systems, Intra operative 
nerve monitors, Deep brain simulators. Transcutaneous electrical nerve 
stimulation (TENS) - applications. Transcranial magnetic stimulation 
(TMS) – applications. Endoscopy – Principles, different types & 
applications. Block diagram of a fibre optic endoscope with integral TV 
cameras.Cryo-surgery techniques and application.
