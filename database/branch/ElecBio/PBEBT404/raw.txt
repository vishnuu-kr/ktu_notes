Project based learning: 9 Hrs 
• 
In the first class, students shall be given an awareness and/ or guidelines on how to 
implement learn this course through a project and form groups of maximum 5 members in 
the first week. 
• 
Each group identify a project where microcontrollers can be used for simple biomedical 
applications and perform a literature survey. 
• 
Choose a suitable microcontroller/ microcontroller- based development boards for your 
application and the input-output devices to be interfaced 
• 
Develop the block diagram and present before the jury within first month after the 
commencement of classes   
•      Implement necessary hardware and software to develop the prototype 
•      Prepare a report and present before the jury for final evaluation.
