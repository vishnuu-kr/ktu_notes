Course Objectives: 
1. This course is designed to offer an in-depth understanding of health informatics, 
blending theoretical frameworks, models, and practical applications within healthcare 
systems. 
2. Students will explore key concepts including electronic health records (EHRs), 
telehealth, health information exchange, and healthcare analytics.
