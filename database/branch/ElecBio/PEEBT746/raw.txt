Course Objectives: 
1. Understanding Deep Learning Architectures: Learn about popular deep learning architectures 
such as AlexNet, VGG16, GoogLeNet, ResNet, and DenseNet. Understand their design 
principles, layer types, and applications. 
2. Hands-on Experience with Deep Learning Platforms: Gain practical experience with deep 
learning platforms like Keras, TensorFlow, and PyTorch. Explore data types, tensor 
operations, model configuration, training, and testing.
