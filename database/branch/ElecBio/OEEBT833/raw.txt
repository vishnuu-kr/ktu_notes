Course Objectives: 
1. To provide an overview of the nature of human visual perception, the elements of an image-
processing system which includes sampling, quantisation and two dimensional transforms. 
2. To make the students familiar with image enhancement, restoration, segmentation, 
classification, machine learning and deep learning techniques for image analysis.

region merging, Boundary-based methods- edge detection and linking,
