Course Objectives: 
1. The objective of this course is to get a basic idea about mechatronics – the working of 
differently powered systems as one unit and also to familiarize the mechanical actuators / 
mechanism and its electrical and electronic supporting systems and along with the application 
of mechatronic systems in industry.

Electrical Actuation Systems, Performance characteristics and System

performance characteristics, Internal sensors, External sensors and 
microsensors. System modelling - Basic building blocks of general 
mechanical, electrical, fluid and thermal systems.

Mechatronic System Controllers: Mechatronic System Controllers (basics 
only): ON/OFF, P, I, D, PI and PID Controllers, Digital controllers,

advantages of NC systems- classification of NC systems - point to point and 
contouring systems - NC and CNC - incremental and absolute systems -open 
loop and closed loop systems.

automation - specification of robots - resolution - repeatability and accuracy
