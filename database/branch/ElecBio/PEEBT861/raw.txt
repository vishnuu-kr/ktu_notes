Course Objectives: 
1. To provide an in-depth understanding of telemedicine systems, their components, and their 
evolution in healthcare. 
2. To analyze the communication technologies used in telemedicine, develop mHealth 
applications, and address data security, ethical issues, and implementation strategies, while 
exploring the broad applications of telemedicine in healthcare.
