Course Objectives: 
1. Understand the principles and applications of electrical machines and power supplies in 
biomedical engineering, including the operation of different types of motors, transformers, 
switched-mode power supplies (SMPS), and special motors. 
2. Apply advanced power electronics knowledge and electrical safety standards in biomedical 
equipment design and implementation, encompassing the construction and characteristics of 
power semiconductor devices, the operation of SCR in controlled rectifiers, and compliance 
with international electrical safety standards.
