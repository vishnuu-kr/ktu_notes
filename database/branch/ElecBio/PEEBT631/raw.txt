Course Objectives: 
1. Apply advanced circuit analysis techniques including mesh and node analysis, and utilize key 
network theorems to both DC and AC circuits, demonstrating problem-solving and critical 
thinking skills 
2. Analyze transient and steady-state responses of RL, RC, and RLC circuits using Laplace 
Transforms and initial condition methods, enhancing students' ability to interpret and evaluate 
circuit behavior. 
3. Design and characterize two-port networks and passive filters, integrating knowledge to 
create effective signal processing solutions.
