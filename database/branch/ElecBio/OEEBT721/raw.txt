Course Objectives: 
1. Understand the principles of bioelectric potentials, including resting and action potentials, and 
their applications in clinical diagnostics such as ECG, EEG, and EMG. 
2. Learn the fundamentals of various biomedical imaging and measurement technologies, such 
as X-ray radiography, MRI, and ultrasound, and their applications in diagnosing and treating 
medical conditions.
