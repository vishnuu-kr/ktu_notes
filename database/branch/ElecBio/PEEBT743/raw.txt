Course Objectives: 
1. 
Student will get the fundamental understanding of BioMEMS, micro-systems and a 
variety of diagnostic and therapeutic applications including intelligent biochips and 
sensors.

Material 
and 
Fabrication: 
Typical 
MEMs 
and 
Microsystems,Compare MEMS and BioMEMS, materials for MEMS -
Silicon and its compounds, Silicon piezo resistors, Gallium Arsenide, 
quartz, polymers. Micromachining techniques-photolithography, thin film 
deposition, doping-diffusion, ion implantation, etching, Bulk machining, 
LIGA process, wafer bonding. 
9 
2 
Sensors And Actuators: Mechanical sensors and actuators – beam and 
cantilever –microplates, strain, pressure and flow measurements. Thermal 
sensors and actuators-actuator based on thermal expansion, thermo 
couples, thermal resistor. Electrostatic sensors and actuators- Parallel 
plate capacitor, pull in effect,Inertia sensor. Piezoelectric sensor and 
actuator: Properties of piezoelectric materials, – inchworm motor, Pressure 
sensor. 
9 
3 
Microfluidic Systems: Fluid dynamics, continuity equation, momentum 
equation, equation of motion, laminar flow in circular conduits, fluid flow 
in micro conduits, fluid actuation methods. Dielectrophoresis, microfluid 
dispenser, microneedle, micro pumps, micro valves.  
9 

 
 
4 
Application of BioMEMS: CAD for MEMS, micro total analysis systems 
(MicroTAS)-Lab-on-a-Chip- detection and measurement, microsystem 
approaches to polymerase chain reaction (PCR). MEMS based drug 
delivery-Drug delivery to the brain, Ocular drug delivery, Gastrointestinal 
tract drug delivery – capsule endoscopy, Transdermal drug delivery, 
Implantable subcutaneous drug delivery. 
9
