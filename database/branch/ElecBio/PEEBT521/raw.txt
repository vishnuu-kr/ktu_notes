Course Objectives: 
1. The basic objective of this course is to introduce the concepts of electronics measurements 
and instrumentation and to provide the comprehensive understanding of principles of 
measurement of basic circuit parameters and electrical quantities using instrumentation, to 
ensure accurate reliable data acquisition in engineering applications.
