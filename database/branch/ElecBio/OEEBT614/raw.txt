Course Objectives: 
1. Study the classification, bonding, crystal structure, mechanical behaviour, and applications of 
metals, polymers, and ceramics in medical implants. 
2. Explore the structure, properties, and applications of collagen-based biomaterials in tissue 
engineering, focusing on mechanical behaviour and tissue regeneration. 
3. Evaluate the factors influencing biocompatibility and blood compatibility of implant 
materials, and apply appropriate testing methods for their design and selection.
