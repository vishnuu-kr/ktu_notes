Course Objectives: 
1. Develop a comprehensive understanding of Internet of Things (IoT) concepts, including 
key protocols, architectural principles, and sensor technologies, with a focus on their 
theoretical applications and implications. 
2. Examine the various IoT protocols and architectures, understanding their roles and 
interactions within IoT ecosystems, and evaluating their effectiveness in different 
contexts. 
3. Explore the theoretical aspects of wearable health monitoring systems, including their 
design, functionality, and integration with data analytics and cloud technologies to 
address healthcare challenges.
