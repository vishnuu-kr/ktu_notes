Course Objectives: 
1. 
To provide students with a foundational knowledge of compiler design, including the need for 
compilers, their phases, and the tools used in their construction. 
2. 
To enable students to design and implement lexical and syntax analyzers using various parsing 
methods, including both top-down and bottom-up approaches. 
3. 
To equip students with the skills to generate intermediate code, optimize code, and develop a basic 
code generator.
