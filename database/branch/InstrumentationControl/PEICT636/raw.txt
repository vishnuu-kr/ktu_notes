Course Objectives: 
1. Study the concepts of total quality management, employee involvement and quality planning. 
2. Study the importance of quality of service ,role of reliability and statistical tools for 
measuring quality. 
3. Identify the importance of continuous process improvement in quality, suppliers, 
benchmarking and quality function deployment. 
4. Identify the importance of total productive maintenance, failure mode and effect analysis, 
need for ISO systems and quality auditing.
