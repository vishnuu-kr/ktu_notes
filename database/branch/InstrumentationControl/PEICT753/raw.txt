Course Objectives: 
1. To expose the students about various instruments used in agriculture 
2. To teach the design of greenhouses using various instruments 
3. To impart ideas about modern technologies used in agriculture

  Soil Science & Sensors 
Soil science :  fundamental definitions & relationships 
properties of soil :  index properties of soil,  Engineering properties 
of soil,  physical properties of soil 
permeability & seepage analysis,  Mohr’s circle of stress 
Necessity of instrumentation & control for agriculture 
Sensors and transducers: definitions,  introduction to sonic 
anemometers,  hygrometers,  thermocouples,  close path gas 
analysers,  introduction to various biosensors

  Instrumentation in industrial Continuous & Batch processes 
Idea about continuous and batch processes 
Flow diagram of sugar plant, sensors & instrumentation set up 
Flow diagram of fermenter, sensors & instrumentation set up 
Flow diagram of dairy industry & instrumentation set up for it 
Juice extraction control process & instrumentation set up.  
Oil extraction plant and instrumentation set up for it. 
Pesticides manufacturing process and control

  Instrumentation in Irrigation 
Necessity of irrigation in agriculture 
Evapotranspiration and its measurement methods 
irrigation methods: overhead, centre pivot, lateral move, micro 
irrigation systems & the comparison of different irrigation systems 
soil moisture measurement methods: resistance-based method, 
voltage based method, thermal based method 
Water distribution & management control, Auto drip & sprinkler 
irrigation systems,  
Irrigation canal management systems 
upstream & downstream control concept 
Introduction to SCADA , Applications of SCADA in agriculture
