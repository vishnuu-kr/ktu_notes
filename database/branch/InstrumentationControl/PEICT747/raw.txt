Course Objectives: 
1. Understand the key components and characteristics of Industry 4.0, including sensors, 
actuators, IoT connectivity, and smart factory technologies. 
2. Develop knowledge of field communication networks, automation protocols, and the 
implementation and architecture of industrial bus systems such as PROFIBUS and 
MODBUS.
