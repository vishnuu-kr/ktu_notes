Course Objectives: 
1. Understand the fundamental concepts and characteristics of biomedical signals. 
2. Understand signal acquisition systems and advanced signal processing techniques. 
3. Apply signal processing knowledge to practical biomedical engineering challenges, 
including cardiological and neurological signal processing.
