Course Objectives: 
1. 
Provide a comprehensive overview of various methods of power generation, including hydro, 
thermal, nuclear, solar, and wind power, with an emphasis on their processes, instrumentation, 
and control mechanisms. 
2. 
Equip students with knowledge about the critical role of instrumentation and control systems in 
power plant operations, including detailed study of boiler and turbine systems, and their 
measurement and control parameters.
