Course Objectives: 
1. To provide students with a comprehensive understanding of industrial process control and 
its applications in various industrial sectors. 
2. To familiarise students with instrumentation symbols and standards as per ISA and the 
control of key industrial processes. 
3. To equip students with the skills to analyse, design, and implement control strategies for 
reactors, distillation towers, heat exchangers, pumps, and compressors.

Control of Pumps and Compressors (9 Hours) 
Control of Pumps: System curves, constant static head, friction pressure 
component, control of centrifugal pumps, head capacity curve, operating 
point of pump, on-off level control, on-off flow control, on-off pressure 
control, throttling flow control, control of rotary pump, on-off control, 
throttling and safety control. 
Control of Centrifugal Compressors: Capacity control method used, curves 
of centrifugal compressor, control of centrifugal 
compressor, suction throttling constant pressure system, suction throttling 
mostly friction system, discharge throttling mostly friction system, surge 
control (fixed setpoint), antisurge control.
