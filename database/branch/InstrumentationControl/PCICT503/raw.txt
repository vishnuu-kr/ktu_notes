Course Objectives: 
1. To provide a thorough understanding of the principles and techniques involved in flow 
measurement, including the various types of flow meters and their applications. 
2. To equip students with the knowledge of fluid mechanics, flow meter linearization and 
compensation, differential pressure flow meters, and control valve systems, enhancing their 
ability to apply this knowledge in practical scenarios.
