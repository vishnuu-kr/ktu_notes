Course Objectives: 
1. To make them understand fundamentals of 4 stroke engine 
2. To expose the students to the concepts of various sensors used in automobile systems. 
3. To teach the basic and advanced controls in automotive systems 
4. To impart ideas on hybrid and electric vehicles, ADAS, GPS

electronic ignition, and EGR for exhaust emission control.
