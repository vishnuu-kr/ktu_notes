Course Objectives: 
 
1. Students will gain expertise in using number systems, Boolean algebra, logic gates 
and minimization techniques 
2. This course aims to equip students with the knowledge to analyse and design both 
combinational and sequential logic circuits using fundamental concepts and practical 
techniques 
3. This course will equip students to independently or collaboratively design and build 
practical digital circuits

Course Objectives: 
 
1. Demonstrate a solid understanding of advanced linear algebra concepts, machine learning 
algorithms and statistical analysis techniques relevant to engineering applications, principles and 
algorithms. 
2. Apply theoretical concepts to solve practical engineering problems, analyze data to extract 
meaningful insights, and implement appropriate mathematical and computational techniques for 
AI and data science applications.
