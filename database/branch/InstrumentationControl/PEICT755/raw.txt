Course Objectives: 
1. 
Gain a comprehensive understanding of IoT technology, including its definitions, architecture, 
functional blocks, communication models, enabling technologies, and deployment templates. 
2. 
Learn about IoT and M2M differences, sensor networks, communication protocols, IoT 
privacy and security solutions, and practical applications such as smart city IoT architecture 
and Raspberry Pi interfacing and programming.
