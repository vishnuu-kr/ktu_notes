Course Objectives: 
1. Develop proficiency in using LabVIEW for graphical system design, virtual instrumentation, 
and modular programming to create, manage, and execute complex engineering processes. 
2. Gain hands-on experience with data acquisition, instrument control, and advanced data 
visualization techniques, integrating hardware and software components effectively for 
comprehensive engineering solutions.
