Course Objectives: 
1. Study the importance of cybersecurity in industries. 
 
2. Understanding the architecture and communication protocols used in ICS.  
3. Conducting risk assessments specific to industrial automation systems. 
4. Developing risk mitigation strategies and contingency plans. 
5. Bridging the gap between Information Technology (IT) and Operational Technology (OT) 
security practices
