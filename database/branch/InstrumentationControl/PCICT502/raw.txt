Course Objectives: 
1. Learn the basics of process control, including process variables, control actions, and 
process control block diagrams. 
2. Study control system objectives, evaluation criteria, and final control operations involving 
signal conversion, actuators, and control elements. 
3. Explore discontinuous and continuous controller modes, including proportional, integral, 
and derivative controls, as well as composite modes like PI, PD, and PID. 
4. Gain proficiency in multiple loop control systems, electronic and pneumatic controllers, 
process loop tuning methods, PLC programming, DCS architecture, and SCADA 
supervisory functions.
