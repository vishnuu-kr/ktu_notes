Course Objectives: 
 
1. Develop a comprehensive understanding of the fundamental concepts of signals and systems, 
including various types of signals and system properties. 
2. Equip students with the ability to mathematically analyze signals and systems using tools 
such as convolution, Fourier series, Fourier transform, Laplace transform, and Z-transform. 
3. Understand the fundamental concepts of sampling and its importance in signal 
processing. 
4. Learn about sampling theorem, aliasing, and reconstruction of signals
