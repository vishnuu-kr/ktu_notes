Course Objectives: 
1. Gain knowledge of various chemical and biological treatment processes, including oxidation, 
reduction, and neutralization, and their control mechanisms. 
2. Learn the control systems and optimization techniques for evaporators, clean rooms, steam 
boilers, and oil industry instrumentation, focusing on maintaining efficiency, safety, and 
environmental compliance.
