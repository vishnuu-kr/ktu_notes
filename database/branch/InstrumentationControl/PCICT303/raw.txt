Course Objectives: 
 
1. Learn various transducer types and their principles. Differentiate between primary and 
secondary transducers. 
2. Understand principles and functions of electrical measuring instruments ,current and 
potential transformers and resistance measurement techniques with bridges and 
ohmmeters. 
3. Master techniques for measuring force, acceleration, torque, power, and fluid 
properties.
