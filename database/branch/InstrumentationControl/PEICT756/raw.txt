Course Objectives: 
1. Understand the fundamental concepts of nonlinear dynamics and chaos. 
2.  Analyze dynamical systems using phase plane techniques. 
3. Explore the behavior of systems near bifurcation points. 
4. Investigate the routes to chaos and the characteristics of chaotic systems
