Course Objectives: 
 
1. Provide students with a comprehensive understanding of integrated circuit fabrication 
techniques and operational amplifier theory. 
2. Enable students to apply their theoretical understanding of operational amplifiers to design 
and analyze fundamental circuit configurations. 
3. To develop a comprehensive understanding of advanced operational amplifier circuits and their 
applications in signal processing, waveform generation, and oscillation. 
4. To comprehensively understand the theory, design principles, and practical 
implementation of various types of analog filters using operational amplifiers. 
5. To develop a comprehensive understanding of analog-to-digital converters (ADCs), digital-
to-analog converters (DACs), voltage regulators, 555 timers, voltage- controlled oscillators 
(VCOs), and phase-locked loops (PLLs)
