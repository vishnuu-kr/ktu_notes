Course Objectives: 
1. Understand the fundamental concepts of electric drives, including types, factors influencing 
selection, thermal characteristics, and load variations for optimal power rating selection. 
2. Gain knowledge of mechanical and speed-torque characteristics of different motors, braking 
methods, starting techniques, and advanced speed control methods for DC and AC drives, 
with practical applications.
