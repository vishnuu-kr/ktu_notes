Course Objectives: 
1. Gain a comprehensive understanding of safety hazards, risks, and the principles of Safety 
Instrumented Systems (SIS), including their design, implementation, and standards 
compliance (IEC 61508, IEC 61511, ISA 84.01-2003). 
2. Develop the ability to identify, assess, and mitigate occupational safety and health hazards, 
utilizing accident theories and models to prevent incidents and ensure a safe working 
environment.
