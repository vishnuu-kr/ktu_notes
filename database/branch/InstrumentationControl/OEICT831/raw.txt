Course Objectives: 
1. Provide an overview of automation concepts, including historical evolution, principles, and 
the importance of automation in modern industries. 
 
2. Introduce students to Programmable Logic Controllers (PLCs) 
3. Explain Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition (SCADA) systems. 
4. Discuss networking fundamentals relevant to industrial automation.
