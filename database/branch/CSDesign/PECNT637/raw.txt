Course Objectives: 
This course helps the learner to understand the stages involved in the design process, the methods 
used by the designers to generate and refine creative ideas, the key considerations that help shape 
them and the feedback and review elements that allow design teams to learn from each job and 
contribute to future commissions. It covers varied skills a learner needs to strategically and creatively 
interpret good design and write for advertising across multiple media platforms.
