Course Objectives: 
1. Enable the learners to understand the fundamental concepts and algorithms in machine 
learning. 
2. To provide a comprehensive understanding about the most popular supervised learning 
algorithms and unsupervised algorithms. 
3. To understand the fundamentals of classification assessment.
