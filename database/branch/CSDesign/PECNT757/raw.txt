Course Objectives: 
This course will help to build an understanding on the basics of optimization techniques and 
introduces basics of linear programming, network flow problems, computational complexity of 
various problems and meta heuristic search technique. The course helps to understand how to 
develop hybrid model to solve an optimization problem.
