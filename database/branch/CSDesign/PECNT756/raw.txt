Course Objectives: 
The purpose of this course is to make awareness about the basic concepts in game and strategies 
involved in the game design. This course helps the learner to understand various design techniques to 
develop new games. The study of computer game design enables the development of algorithms for 
creating various games. This is the course for awarding B.Tech. Minor in Computer Science and Design 
with specialization in Game Design.
