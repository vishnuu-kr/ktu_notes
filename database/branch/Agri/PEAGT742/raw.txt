Course Objectives: 
1. To acquaint and equip the students with processing and handling of fruits and vegetables and 
flowers 
2. To minimize the post-harvest losses through value addition and post-harvest unit operations 
3. To facilitate the design features of the equipment used for the processing of horticultural products 
4. To understand various preservation, packaging and storage methods of horticultural produce 
5. To expose the students to modern facilities in supply chain management of horticultural products
