Course Objectives: 
1. To understand the transformation of raw materials into consumables.  
2. To learn the fundamentals of innovative techniques, technological advancements and culinary 
expertise in modern food industries. 
3. To provide the knowledge and skills needed to meet the dynamic demands of the food industry
