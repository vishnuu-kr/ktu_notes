Course Objectives: 
1. To understand the cutting-edge field of Mechatronics with a specialized focus on its applications 
in agriculture.  
2. To learn the integration of mechanical systems, electronics, computer science, and control 
engineering is revolutionizing farming practices.  
3. To understand how mechatronics is enhancing efficiency, sustainability, and precision in modern 
agriculture, paving the way for innovative solutions to address the challenges of the agricultural 
industry.
