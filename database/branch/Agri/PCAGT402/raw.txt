Course Objectives: 
1. To understand the importance of soil mechanics in engineering, index properties and 
its determination. 
 
2. To understand effective pressure of soil, permeability and compressibility of soil 
3. To analyse stress distribution, shear strength and lateral earth pressure of soil 
4. To evaluate stability of infinite and finite slope 
5. To understand the bearing capacity of soil and different kinds of foundations used in 
engg constructions

Bearing capacity of soils: Definitions related to foundations and 
bearing capacity, Minimum depth of foundation - Rankine's 
analysis, type of bearing capacity failures, effect of water table on
