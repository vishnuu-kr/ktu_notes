Course Objectives: 
1. To identify and enumerate different mechanisms and analyse velocity and 
accelerations of the links involved in the mechanism.  
2. To familiarize design and strength requirements of various power transmission 
elements including cam and follower, belt drives, bearings, coupling, keys, joints and 
bolts.

diagrams, principle of virtual work. Force principle: D'Alembert's Principle,
