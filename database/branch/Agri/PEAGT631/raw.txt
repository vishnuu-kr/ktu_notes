Course Objectives: 
1. To understand the importance and role of renewable sources of energy in the energy sector  
 
2. To understand the production of energy through solar photovoltaic system 
3. To learn about wind energy and turbines 
4. To understand various aspects of bio energy and bio fuels 
5. To understand the characteristics and basic principle of green technology
