Course Objectives: 
1. To acquaint and equip the students with the basic principles of irrigation and Soil-Plant Water 
relations  
2. Understand different kinds of irrigation systems and water control and diversion structures and to 
introduce the concept of command area development 
3. To introduce and impart the design concepts of micro-irrigation and Sprinkler irrigation systems.  
4. Understand the basic concepts for planning, design and management of land drainage works in 
cultivated areas

WATER RESOURCES AND IRRIGATION REQUIREMENT 
Water Resources- Introduction to irrigation; land grading and levelling-
estimation of earth work; Soil water plant relationship, soil water 
movement, infiltration; Determination of evapotranspiration-Crop water 
requirement, irrigation requirement, irrigation scheduling and irrigation 
efficiencies; Introduction to different types of irrigation.

Surface and Subsurface methods of Irrigation-Design of lined and unlined 
canals-Silt theories- Kennedy’s and Lacey’s theories, Lining of water courses 
and field channels; Underground pipeline irrigation system design. Water 
conveyance -Water control and diversion structures; Command area - 
Concept, Components of CADP, Classification of canals- Alignment of 
canals. 
9  
3 
MICRO IRRIGATION AND SPRINKLER IRRIGATION   
Micro Irrigation, components -Drip, micro sprinkler, mist, fogger etc.; 
Design of drip irrigation system; Maintenance of micro irrigation systems;  
Sprinkler irrigation, types of sprinkler irrigation systems; design of 
sprinkler irrigation system: layout selection.   Performance evaluation of 
sprinkler irrigation system: uniformity coefficient and pattern efficiency; – 
Determination of pump capacity from head and discharge, selection and 
installation of pumps. 
9 
4 
AGRICULTURAL DRAINAGE 
Agricultural drainage –Factors affecting drainage; Drainable porosity, 
Drainage coefficient; Principles of flow through soils, Darcy’s law; Surface 
drainage systems - Subsurface drainage - Design of subsurface drainage -
Steady state equations- Pipe materials - mole drains, drainage wells, 
Leaching requirements - irrigation and drainage water quality.
