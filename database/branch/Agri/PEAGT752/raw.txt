Course Objectives: 
1. To acquaint and equip the students with different unit operations of food industries and their 
design features.  
2. To develop skills to process and handle fruits and vegetables, minimizing post-harvest losses and 
understanding equipment design features for processing. 
3. To equip students with post-harvest technology for major cereals, pulses, and oilseeds, 
emphasizing equipment

Rice milling, parboiling, methods:traditional and modern methods, milling

objectives; blanching methods, effects on food (nutrition, colour, pigment, 
texture), Physiological changes during ripening, maturity index, firmness, sugar
