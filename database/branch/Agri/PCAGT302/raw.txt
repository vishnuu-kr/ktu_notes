Course Objectives: 
1. To make the students understand the basic concepts of stress, strain, and elasticity, and their 
applications in analyzing the behavior of materials under various loading conditions. 
2. To develop the ability to analyze the mechanical behavior of structural elements including beams, 
columns, and shafts subjected to axial, bending, torsional, and combined loading. 
3. To provide an insight into the principles of structural design, including selecting appropriate 
materials, sizing structural members, and ensuring structural safety and serviceability. 
4. To make the students learn the methods to analyze deflection and stability of structural elements 
under different loading conditions, and understand their implications for structural design.
