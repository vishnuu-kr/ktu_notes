Course Objectives: 
1. To acquire knowledge and skills in designing reinforced concrete structures, including beams, 
columns, foundations, and connections. 
2. To study the different types of structural systems (such as beams, columns, slabs, frames, and 
trusses) and their applications in building and infrastructure projects. 
3. To understand the design of simple steel structures 
 
4. To learn the principles of designing farm structures, including considerations such as structural 
stability, environmental factors (like wind and snow loads), ventilation, lighting, and animal 
comfort. 
5. To develop skills in estimating the costs associated with designing and constructing farm 
structures, considering factors such as materials, labour, equipment, and overhead costs.
