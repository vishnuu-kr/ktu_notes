Course Objectives: 
1. To understand the physical behavior of the various modes of heat transfers 
2. To understand the concepts of heat transfer through extended surfaces. 
3. To develop understanding about the methods of determination of heat transfer rates in 
conduction, convection and radiation. 
4. To learn the thermal analysis and sizing of heat exchangers  
5. To understand the basic concepts of mass transfer and its applications

CONDUCTION 
Basic concepts – Mechanism of heat transfer – Conduction, convection and 
radiation – Thermal properties of materials and measurement - General 
differential equation of heat conduction – Fourier law of conduction – 
Cartesian and cylindrical coordinates – one dimensional steady state heat 
conduction – Conduction through plane walls, cylinders and spherical 
systems – Composite systems – Conduction with internal heat generation –

CONVECTION 
Basic concepts – Convective heat transfer coefficients – Boundary Layer 
concept – Types of convection – Forced convection – Dimensional analysis 
– Useful non dimensional numbers– External flow – Flow over plates, 
Cylinders and spheres – Internal flow – Laminar and turbulent flow – 
Combined Laminar and turbulent flow – Flow over bank of tubes – Free 
convection - Dimensional analysis – Flow over vertical plates, horizontal 
plate, inclined plate, cylinders and spheres.

RADIATION 
Basic concepts of radiation, Absorptivity, Reflectivity and Transmittivity of 
radiation,law of radiation – Stefan Boltsmann law, Kirchoff law – Black body 
radiation – Grey body radiation - shape factor algebra – solid angle, intensity 
of radiation. Radiation exchange between black surfaces, geometric 
configuration factor, Electrical analogy – Radiation shields – introduction to 
gas radiation
