Course Objectives: 
1. To expose the students to the fundamental knowledge of food, its properties and different 
methods of food processing  
2. To understand the chemical, physical, and biological properties of milk and dairy products 
3. To learn the methods and technologies used in the processing of milk and the production of 
various dairy products such as cheese, butter and ice cream. 
4. To gain knowledge about the design, operation, and maintenance of equipment and machinery 
used in the dairy and food industry, including pasteurizers, sterilizers, homogenizers, separators, 
evaporators, filtration and dryers. 
5. To equip the students to design and layout of dairy plant, sanitation and effluent treatment in 
dairy industry
