Course Objectives: 
1. To provide a comprehensive overview of the automotive industry and the historical evolution 
of automobiles, focusing on internal combustion engine (ICE) vehicles and their 
classifications. 
2. To introduce and elaborate on the various systems and technologies used in modern 
automobiles, including fuel, ignition, lubrication, cooling, exhaust, power trains, suspension, 
brakes, and electronic systems.

Constructional details, Single plate and multi-plate. Numerical calculations

of automatic transmission (AMT, CVT, DCT, TC – an overview).
