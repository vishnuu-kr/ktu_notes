Course Objectives: 
1. Learn how to program CNC machines and control automated equipment with PLC. 
2. To introduce machining principles and processes in the manufacturing of precision components 
and products that use non-conventional technologies. 
3. To offer a basic understanding of machining capabilities, limitations, and productivity of 
advanced manufacturing techniques.
