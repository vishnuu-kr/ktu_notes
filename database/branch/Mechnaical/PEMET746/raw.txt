Course Objectives: 
1. Students will able to learn about lean manufacturing, types of industrial wastes associated and 
tools for eliminating the wastes. 
2. Students will understand about the lean elements and identify those within industries. 
3. Students can understand the concept of six sigma and agile manufacturing.
