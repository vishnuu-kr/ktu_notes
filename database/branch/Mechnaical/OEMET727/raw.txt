Course Objectives: 
1. To develop a comprehensive understanding of steam, gas, hydro and nuclear power plants and 
various energy storage systems. 
2. To familiarise various terms related to power plant economics.

Analysis of Steam Cycle 
Steam engineering-temperature entropy diagram- mollier diagram- Rankine 
cycle-steam power plant, internally irreversible and externally irreversible

Energy Storage 
Pumped hydro, Compressed air energy storage, flywheel energy storage,
