Course Objectives: 
1. To develop a comprehensive understanding of the fundamental principles and theories involved 
in design. 
2. To familiarize students with international standards and codes. 
3. To teach the systematic approach to design. 
4. To enable students to select appropriate materials based on mechanical properties. 
5. To analyze and predict potential failure modes.
