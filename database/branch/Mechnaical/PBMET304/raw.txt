Course Objectives: 
 
1. To gain theoretical and practical knowledge in manufacturing processes and to develop and 
understanding of the dependent and independent variables which control a production 
processes. 
2. Provide a detailed discussion on the welding process and the physics of welding. Introduce 
students to different welding processes weld testing and advanced processes to be able to 
appreciate the practical applications of welding. 
3. Generate solutions to problems that may arise in manufacturing engineering
