Course Objectives: 
1. To provide a foundational understanding of CAD, including its historical evolution, industrial 
applications, and key components such as geometric and solid modeling, CAD/CAM/CAE/CAPP 
packages, hardware, and software developments. 
2. To develop proficiency in 2D and 3D computer graphics, focusing on transformations, drawing 
algorithms, solid modeling techniques, and coordinate systems, alongside foundational 
knowledge in finite element method (FEM) and finite volume method (FVM).
