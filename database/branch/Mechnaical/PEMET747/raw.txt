Course Objectives: 
1. To induce an attitude towards reliability engineering in students which will ensure that they 
can identify steps to avoid failures in their future assignments.  
2. To understand the basic principles of reliability engineering and its applications to various 
systems in engineering

Reliability function, MTTF and MTBF, Hazard rate function, Bathtub curve,

System Analysis and Reliability Estimation: Fault tree analysis, Event tree

Software reliability: Software errors; Fault Tolerance; Data Reliability;
