Course Objectives: 
1. 
This course helps the student to the basic idea of Robots. Students are introduced to the 
basic design considerations of robots.  
2. 
Concepts like trajectory planning and obstacle avoidance and kinematics of robots are 
introduced.  
3. 
Discussion on various mobile robots and robotic manipulators are also included as part 
of the course to get an overall idea on robotics
