Course Objectives: 
1. To get an awareness about the usefulness of numerical methods in solving different 
engineering problems  
2. To understand the error involved in numerical analysis

Motivation and Applications of numerical methods, Significant digits,

Numerical integration-Trapezoidal, Simpson’s one-third rule, Simpson’s
