Course Objectives: 
1. To provide students with an understanding of IoT architecture, protocols, and integration 
techniques that enable device-to-device, device-to-cloud, and cloud-to-cloud communications. 
2. To enable students with the ability to create and implement IoT solutions using platforms like 
Raspberry Pi, cloud-based services, and analytics tools to develop real-world IoT applications.
