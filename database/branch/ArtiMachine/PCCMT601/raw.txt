Course Objectives: 
1. To introduce fundamental NLP tasks, applications, and language processing challenges. 
2. To explore basic machine learning techniques, including neural networks and transformers, used in 
NLP. 
3. To impart rule-based and machine learning approaches, emphasizing pre-trained models and transfer 
learning.
