Course Objectives: 
1. To give the  foundational principles and history of computational linguistics and key 
linguistic subfields. 
2. To enable the learners to develop skills to create and analyse models for syntactic and 
semantic tasks, focusing on ambiguity resolution. 
3. To provide the learners an experience in designing and implementing NLP applications 
using modern computational techniques.
