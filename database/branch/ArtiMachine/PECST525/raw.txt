Course Objectives: 
1. To provide a thorough understanding of the key processes and concepts involved in data 
mining and data warehousing within application domains 
2. To enable students to understand the different data preprocessing techniques, 
fundamentals and advanced concepts of classification, clustering, association rule 
mining, text mining and web mining, and apply these techniques in real-world 
scenarios
