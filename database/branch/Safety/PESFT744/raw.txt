Course Objectives: 
1. Understand the types, causes, effects, and analysis of natural and man-made disasters. 
2. Explore the principles and practices of disaster preparedness, response, and the role of 
various stakeholders. 
3. Analyze the rehabilitation, reconstruction, and recovery processes after a disaster, 
including long-term planning.
