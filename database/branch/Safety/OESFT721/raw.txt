Course Objectives: 
1. Explain sustainable development and different environmental agreements and protocols 
2. Discuss real time activities causing environmental issues and different methods to use 
renewable energy resources  
3. Explain local and global environmental issues  
4. Differentiate between carbon emissions for regular and sustainable cities and explain different 
practices to move industries towards sustainability  
5. Discuss different renewable energy resources and explain methods to implement green 
technology
