Course Objectives: 
1. To learn about the principle of fire extinguishment and the performance of extinguishing 
agent.  
2. To understand the principle of different automatic fire detection systems and their application.  
3. To learn about the different types of first aid and portable fire protection system; their 
operation and maintenance requirements.  
4. To learn about the details and design principle of fixed firefighting systems with different 
extinguishers like water, CO2, DCP and foam.  
5. To learn about different types of fire alarm system and sprinkler systems.

Fire protection Engineering - function of fire protection system, performance 
based fire protection system, prescriptive fire protection design. Principles of 
Fire Extinguishments - extinction of premixed flames, diffusion flames and 
burning metals, fire triangle, fire tetrahedron. Basic concept of fire fighting 
with water, carbon dioxide, powders, foams, inert gases halons. Need for 
halon replacement and halon substitutes, clean agent system design. 
Extinguishant performance - flame extinguishing concentration, inerting 
concentration, fire trials.

Fire detection - Need and importance of automatic fire detection system, 
principle of detection, classification of detectors. Heat detectors – fixed 
temperature, rate of rise, thermistor rate of rise and rate compensated type 
detectors. Smoke detectors - optical and ionization type, photo electric light 
scattering and light obstruction type detectors. Flame detectors - infra red 
and ultra violet detectors. Flammable gas detection - Pellistor and laser

Design of detection system-design fire curve-plunge test-heat detection

3 
First aid fire protection – fire bucket, sand bucket, fire blanket, fire pails & 
water barrels, hose reels. Description, working principle, method of 
operation of different types of portable fire extinguishers-water type, foam 
type and limitations, dry powder type, CO2 type, vapourizing liquid type. 
Placement of extinguishers. Extinguisher rating, Care, inspection, and 
maintenance of portable extinguishers as per relevant Indian standards 
specifications.  
Automatic water sprinkler system- risk categories, requirement and source of 
water supply, automatic pumps. Automatic sprinkler heads - Quartzoid 
type,fusible link type, and modern types. Mounting and protection of 
sprinkler heads. Sprinkler pipe works-standard and staggered lay out, 
hangers. Control valves for wet and dry installations. Deluge valve. Pre –
action. Drenchers. High velocity and medium velocity spray system. 
Principles of water sprinkler system design as per relevant standards (ISI). 
Automatic fire sprinkler calculations-hydraulic calculations, area-density 
curve. Sprinkler design considerations for warehouse storage in a 
manufacturing plant. Fire protection of specialised occupancies-computer 
rooms-communication centre-airport.
