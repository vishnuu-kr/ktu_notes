Course Objectives: 
1. To familiarize the concept of heat conduction, convection and radiation  
2. To describe the application of different types of heat exchangers used in industries.  
3. To illustrate with examples the different types of evaporators used in chemical industries. 
4. To understand the principle of mass transfer, theories and application.  
5. To describe absorbers with the help of relevant examples. 
6. To compute number of stages in a distillation column with the help of McCabe Thiele 
method. 
7. To describe drying and extraction principles with their application.

Project based learning (8 Hours) 
1. Direct the students to select a heat exchanger or evaporator. The faculty may ensure that it is 
suitable for the course. The working should have been analysed for heat transfer calculations, 
energy balance and applicability of laws. 
2. Students should prepare the analysis report before completion of the I module. 
3. Students should use appropriate software for the simulation of the operation parameters. 
4. Design the process modifications if any. 
5. Design the heat exchanger. 
6. Prepare a report. 
7. Demonstrate/present the results.
