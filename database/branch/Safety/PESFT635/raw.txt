Course Objectives 
1. Learn the foundational concepts of functional safety and relevant standards (IEC 
61508, ISO 26262, IEC 61511) for E/E/PE systems. 
2. Develop skills to assess risks and determine Safety Integrity Levels (SIL) using 
methods like HAZOP, Bow Tie Analysis, and LOPA. 
3. Gain proficiency in designing, implementing, and maintaining Safety Instrumented 
Systems (SIS), including failure management, safety lifecycle, and verification 
processes.
