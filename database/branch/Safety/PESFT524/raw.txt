Course Objectives: 
1. Understand the fundamental concepts and theories of human and organizational factors in 
safety. 
2. Analyze the impact of human behavior, decision-making, and cognitive processes on safety 
performance. 
3. Evaluate the influence of organizational culture, leadership, and management practices on 
safety outcomes. 
4. Develop strategies for designing and implementing effective safety management systems.
