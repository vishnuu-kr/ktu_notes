Course Objectives: 
1. To enable students to apply the knowledge of combustion to fire propagation and control 
2. To emphasize the basics of the subject of fire, its physical characteristics and modelling. 
3. To explain the mathematics involved in transport processes during fire propagation as well as the 
empirical correlations for fire spread and fuel burning rates in detail.
