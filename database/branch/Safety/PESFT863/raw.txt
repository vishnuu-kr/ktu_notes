Course Objectives: 
1. Learn the basics of project management, key terms and definitions, and roles and 
responsibilities 
2. Learn how to start, plan, and manage projects, including how to schedule tasks, manage time, 
and control costs 
3. Learn how to evaluate and manage risk factors, and establish processes for managing 
uncertainty 
4. Learn how to achieve project goals within constraints, and ensure customer satisfaction 
5. Learn how to align project plans with an organization's business model and strategic goals
