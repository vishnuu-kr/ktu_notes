Course Objectives: 
1. To impart knowledge about various tools and techniques of Industrial Engineering. 
2. To facilitate students to acquire knowledge about inventory management, lean manufacturing, 
agile manufacturing, enterprise resource planning and thus inculcate the skills needed to apply 
these principles in an organization. 
3. To get acquainted with quality management practices
