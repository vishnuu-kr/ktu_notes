Course Objectives: 
1. This course provides an overview of the Sustainability through Green Manufacturing Systems; 
various methodologies and its application in improving the eco-efficiency are focused.  
2. Students will also learn about the commonly used Sustainable manufacturing tools such as 
Environmentally Conscious Quality Function Deployment (ECQFD) and Life Cycle Assessment 
(LCA).

Course Objectives: 
1. To provide basic knowledge on accidents and their impacts on construction; safety 
legislations and rules to be followed in construction; site safety practices to be followed 
during construction practices; human factors; ergonomics and cost of accidents and make 
aware on role of various parties for site safety management.

Confined Place - Prevention from Drowning - Fire Prevention and Protection 
- First Aid and Medical Care.
