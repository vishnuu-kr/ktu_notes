Course Objectives: 
1. To provide an in-depth understanding of maritime safety regulations, standards, and best 
practices. 
2. To explore the technical and engineering aspects of safety systems, equipment, and 
procedures on board ships. 
3. To critically analyze safety challenges, risk assessment techniques, and human factors in 
maritime operations. 
4. To develop problem-solving skills related to designing and managing safety systems in 
compliance with international maritime standards.
