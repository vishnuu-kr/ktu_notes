Course Objectives: 
1. To equip the student with the expertise to computationally model real life problems and 
subsequently educate the student to solve these models with the help of the available 
statistical and numerical methods. The mathematical concepts and models deal with solving 
engineering problems using minimum available resources.  
2. To apply classical, numerical and modern methods for solving engineering problems. 
3. To get an awareness about the usefulness of numerical and statistical methods in solving 
different engineering problems.
