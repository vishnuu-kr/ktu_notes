Course Objectives 
1. To give basic idea about the fundamentals of chemical process safety 
2. To provide basic knowledge about toxicity and Industrial Hygiene.  
3. To impart ideas on source models, explosion protections and testing and commissioning of 
process plants

Project based learning (8Hours) 
1. Direct the students to select a simple chemical manufacturing process. The faculty may 
ensure that it is suitable for the course. The process should have been analysed for inherent 
safety,design for safety,etc. 
2. Students should prepare the analysis report before completion of the I module. 
3. Students should use appropriate software for the simulation of the process. 
4. Design the process modifications if any. 
5. Apply design principles in the process. 
6. Prepare a report. 
7. Demonstrate/present the results.
