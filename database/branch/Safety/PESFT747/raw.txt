Course Objectives: 
1. Understand the fundamentals and challenges of successful product development and design 
processes. 
2. Learn product planning methods, customer needs identification, and techniques for concept 
generation and selection. 
3. Apply tools like DFMEA/PFMEA, product architecture strategies, and industrial design 
principles to enhance product quality. 
4. Develop skills in prototyping, and product development economics to optimize design 
efficiency and cost-effectiveness.
