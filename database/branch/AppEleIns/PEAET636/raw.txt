Course Objectives: 
1. To introduce the fundamental concepts of biomedical imaging and its analysis. 
2. To understand various medical imaging modalities and their applications. 
3. To learn techniques for image enhancement, segmentation, and analysis. 
4. To apply image analysis techniques to real-world biomedical problems. 
5. To gain practical experience through laboratory exercises and projects.
