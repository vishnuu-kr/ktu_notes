Course Objective: 
1. To understand the physical processes and working principles of semiconductor devices, while 
relating the device performance to material parameters and design criteria.

Review of Semiconductor physics - Equilibrium and steady state 
conditions, Concept of effective mass and Fermi level, Density of states &
