Course Objectives: 
1. To introduce the fundamentals of blockchain technology and its relevance to the automotive 
industry. 
2. To explore the potential applications of blockchain in various aspects of the automotive sector, 
including supply chain, manufacturing, and vehicle maintenance. 
3. To equip students with the knowledge to analyze and implement blockchain solutions in 
automotive engineering contexts.
