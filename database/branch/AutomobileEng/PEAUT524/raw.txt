Course Objectives: 
1. To provide students with a comprehensive understanding of the fundamental concepts of 
production and operations management specifically tailored to the automotive industry. 
2. To equip students with the skills to strategically plan production activities in automotive 
manufacturing. 
3. To enable students to analyse and manage supply chain operations, apply quality management 
principles and environmental sustainability practices to enhance efficiency and competitiveness 
in the automotive sector.
