Course Objectives: 
1. To understand engine and vehicle testing methods, focusing on performance, energy, and fuel 
consumption. 
2. To understand crashworthiness and perform comprehensive impact tests using advanced crash 
test techniques. 
3. To analyse aerodynamic analysis and control vehicle noise and vibration through wind tunnel 
testing and NVH techniques. 
4. To analyse ride quality and safety using vibration measurement and comprehensive wheel and 
brake performance tests.
