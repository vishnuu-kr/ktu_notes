Course Objectives: 
1. Understand automotive battery technologies, charging, and starting systems. 
2. Learn about ignition systems, fuel injection types, and their applications. 
3. Explore automotive lighting, instrumentation, wiring systems, diagnostics, sensors, 
actuators, and IOT integration

motor; Starter Motor types, construction and characteristics, Starter
