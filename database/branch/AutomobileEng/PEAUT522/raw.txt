Course Objectives: 
1. To understand pollutant sources, formation mechanisms, environmental impacts, and 
compliance with international emission standards. 
2. To demonstrate proficiency in analyzing the formation of pollutants like NOx, CO, 
particles, and hydrocarbons in engines, and applying emission measurement procedures 
for vehicles. 
3. Apply knowledge of emission control technologies in gasoline and diesel vehicles, 
evaluate inspection procedures, and assess vehicle replacement and retrofit programs 
to meet emission standards.

Introduction: Pollutants – sources – formation – effects of pollution on 
environment – human. Emission standards & regulations –International 
standards, US, European union standards, & Indian standards. Compliance 
with standards – certification - assembly lane testing – In use surveillance & 
recall. 
 
9 
2 
Mechanism of pollutant formation – Formation of NOx & CO in SI and CI 
engines – formation of particles in SI and CI engines – oxidation of soot – role 
of soot inhibitors Formation of HC – aldehydes, ketones, alcohols, and organic 
acids - formation of HC in SI and Diesel Engines. Non-Exhaust Emissions -
Emission measurement and testing procedures – light and heavy-duty vehicles 
& two wheelers. Crankcase, evaporative, refuelling and on-road emissions. 
NDIR analysers, FID, Chemiluminescence analysers etc. 
 
9 

 
 
3 
Technology for controlling emissions – Gasoline fuelled vehicles – A/F ratio, 
electronic control. Catalytic convertors – two way and three way convertors – 
catalytic wear and poisoning. Diesel fuelled vehicles – engine design – exhaust 
after treatment – Particulate trap  EGR- Crankcase emission and control - 
evaporative emission and control – fuel dispensing and distribution emissions 
and control, SCR, diesel oxidation catalyst (DOC) Diesel Exhaust Fluid 
(DEF),

Controlling emission - inspection procedures for SI engines – exhaust & 
evaporative emissions – motorcycle white smoke. Institutional setting for 
inspection and maintenance – centralized and decentralized I/M – comparison 
– inspection frequency and roadside inspection Vehicle replacement and 
retrofit programs – scrap age and relocation – replacement – retrofit program.
