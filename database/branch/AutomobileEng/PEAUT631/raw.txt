Course Objectives: 
1. Learn about different types of two and three-wheelers, including their design, 
suspension, steering, and powertrain technologies. 
2. Gain skills in analysing transmission, drive systems, braking mechanisms, and 
components of electric vehicles. 
3. Develop proficiency in vehicle maintenance, safety standards, and emerging 
technologies in vehicle design and smart systems.
