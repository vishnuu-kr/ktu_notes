Course Objectives: 
1. Understand the fundamental principles of refrigeration and air conditioning systems, including 
thermodynamics, cycle efficiency, and types of refrigerants used. 
2. Understand and classify various air conditioning systems, components, and their operational 
principles, focusing on their layout and impact on automotive and building environments. 
3. Understand and optimize air distribution systems, including duct sizing, ventilation, and control 
mechanisms. 
4. Develop proficiency in diagnosing, servicing, and maintaining air conditioning systems.
