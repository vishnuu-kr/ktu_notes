Course Objectives: 
1. To understand the modern technologies automotive technologies like Electric, hybrid and fuel cell 
vehicle technology, different components associated with these systems. 
2. To understand the various ADAS technologies such as adaptive cruise control, lane-keeping 
assist, and automated parking systems. 
3. To understand the automobile navigation systems, connected vehicles and autonomous driving 
cars.
