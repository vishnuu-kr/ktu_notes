Course Objectives: 
1. Understand and compare CAD technologies with traditional methods of designing, focusing on 
advantages such as efficiency, precision, and cost-effectiveness 
2. Explore different topologies of CAD systems, including design databases, graphic standards, and 
data exchange formats, and understand their role in facilitating efficient design processes 
3. Acquire practical skills in 2D graphics algorithms, transformations and grasp fundamental concepts 
in 3D graphic
