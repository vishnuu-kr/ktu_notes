Course Objectives: 
1. Understand the performance characteristics and vibration behaviour of road vehicles, including 
the effects of tractive resistance, tractive effort, and power requirements 
2. Comprehend the dynamics of tires and braking systems, focusing on tire behaviour during 
cornering, braking dynamics, and the impact of tire characteristics on vehicle performance 
3. Analyze the stability of vehicles under various conditions, considering load distribution, 
gyroscopic effects, and the impact of aerodynamic forces on road loads and vehicle handling
