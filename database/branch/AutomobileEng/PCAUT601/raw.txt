Course Objectives: 
1. Understand the fundamental principles and components of hybrid and fuel cell vehicle 
technologies, including their energy conversion mechanisms and powertrain configurations.
 
 
2. Understand the environmental and societal impacts of hybrid and fuel cell vehicles, comparing 
their advantages and challenges with conventional internal combustion engine vehicles.
