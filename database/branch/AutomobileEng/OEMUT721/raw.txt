Course Objectives: 
1. Understand the fundamental principles and components of Electric and Hybrid vehicle 
technologies, including their energy conversion mechanisms and powertrain configurations.
 
 
2. Understand the environmental and societal impacts of Electric and Hybid vehicles, comparing 
their advantages and challenges with conventional internal combustion engine vehicles.
