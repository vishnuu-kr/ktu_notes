Course Objectives: 
1. Understand about kinematic pairs, chains, and degrees of freedom, and analyze 4-bar and 
slider crank chains for velocity and acceleration. 
2. Explore the types and functions of governors, cams, and followers, and analyze their motion 
characteristics and cam profile generation. 
3. Gain insights into gears, gear trains, flywheels, and the balancing of rotating and 
reciprocating masses, including the principles of gyroscopic effects in vehicle stability.
