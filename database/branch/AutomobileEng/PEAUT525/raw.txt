Course Objectives: 
1. Comprehend the principles of operation, emf equations, and characteristics of shunt and series 
excited DC generators and motors, along with their torque-speed equations and industry 
applications. 
2. Understand the principles of transformer operations, including emf equations, vector diagrams, 
and efficiency calculations, by conducting and interpreting OC and SC tests. 
3. Understand the operation principles of single-phase motors and synchronous machines, 
including their types, regulation methods, and starting mechanisms. 
4. Study the operation and control methods of stepper motors and gain insights into various 
automation controllers, such as servo controls, digital controllers, and programmable logic 
controllers.
