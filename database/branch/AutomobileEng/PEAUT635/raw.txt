Course Objectives: 
1. To provide a comprehensive understanding of vehicle maintenance principles and practices for 
conventional, electric, and hybrid vehicles. 
2. To equip students with practical skills for diagnosing, servicing, and maintaining various 
vehicle systems and components. 
3. To familiarize students with advanced diagnostic tools, safety practices, and emerging trends 
in vehicle maintenance technology.
