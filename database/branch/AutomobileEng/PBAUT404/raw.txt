Course Objectives: 
 
1. Understand the basic concept of foundry and casting, metal forming and welding. 
2. Understand different defects formed in the manufacturing process. 
3. Select the proper manufacturing process for the given applications. 
4. Understand the basics of advanced manufacturing technology.
