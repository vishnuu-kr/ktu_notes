Course Objectives: 
1. To understand the principles and components of autonomous driving and ADAS technologies. 
2. To explore the various sensor technologies and their integration into autonomous and ADAS 
systems. 
3. To analyse the role of AI, machine learning, and software platforms in autonomous driving.

Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning - Role of AI in

simulation tools.

Cloud Computing and Edge Processing - Role of cloud computing in

handling, Examples of cloud-based autonomous driving platforms
