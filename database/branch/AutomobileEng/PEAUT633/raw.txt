Course Objectives: 
1. To provide a comprehensive understanding of the principles and practices of vehicle 
transport and fleet management. 
2. To equip students with the skills necessary to manage and optimize fleet operations, 
including maintenance, fuel management, and safety compliance. 
3. To introduce advanced technologies and strategic planning techniques used in modern 
fleet management to enhance efficiency and sustainability.
